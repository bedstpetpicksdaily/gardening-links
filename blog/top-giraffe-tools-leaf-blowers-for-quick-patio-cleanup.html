Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Giraffe Tools leaf blower battery last?

Battery runtime varies by model and usage intensity, typically ranging from 20-60 minutes on a full charge. Most homeowners find this sufficient for standard patio and yard cleanup. The brand offers compatible batteries across their outdoor power tool line for extended operation.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for wet leaves?

Giraffe Tools leaf blowers work best on dry leaves and debris. For wet leaves, it's recommended to let them dry first or use a leaf vacuum model that can handle slightly damp material. Check your specific model's specifications for moisture handling capability.

Can Giraffe Tools leaf blowers be used for snow clearing?

While leaf blowers can move light, dry snow, Giraffe Tools offers dedicated snow blowers for heavy winter cleanup. For light snow maintenance on patios and decks, a leaf blower can help, but dedicated snow equipment provides better performance for significant accumulation.

How do I maintain my Giraffe Tools leaf blower?

Maintenance is minimal with electric models. Store in a dry location, keep the air intake clean, and charge the battery according to manufacturer instructions. Periodically inspect the motor housing and air vents for debris, and always follow the user manual for specific care recommendations.
