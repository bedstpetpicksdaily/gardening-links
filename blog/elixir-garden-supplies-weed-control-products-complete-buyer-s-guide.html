Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between selective and non-selective weed killers?

Selective weed killers target specific weed types while preserving desirable plants, making them ideal for lawns. Non-selective herbicides kill most vegetation they contact, suitable for clearing large areas or preparing beds. Elixir Garden Supplies stocks both types for different applications.

How long does it take to see results from Elixir Garden Supplies weed control products?

Most Elixir weed control products show visible results within 3-7 days, though complete eradication may take longer for persistent weeds. Always follow product instructions for optimal timing and application methods.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies weed control products safe for vegetables?

This depends on the specific product. Elixir offers organic and selective options safe for vegetable gardens. Always read the product label carefully and follow safety guidelines regarding application near edible plants.

Can I use Elixir weed control products in wet weather?

Most weed control products work best in dry conditions. Check your specific Elixir product's instructions, as some formulations are more weather-resistant than others. Rain shortly after application can reduce effectiveness.
