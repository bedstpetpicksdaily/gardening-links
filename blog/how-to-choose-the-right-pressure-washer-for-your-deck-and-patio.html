Understanding PSI and GPM: The Core Specifications

When shopping for a pressure washer, you'll encounter two main specifications: PSI (pounds per square inch) and GPM (gallons per minute). PSI measures the force of the water spray, while GPM indicates the volume of water dispensed. For deck and patio cleaning, you typically need between 1500-3000 PSI, which is gentle enough to avoid damaging wood surfaces while powerful enough to remove dirt and debris effectively. A GPM rating of 1.5-2.0 is ideal for residential applications.

Giraffe Tools pressure washers are engineered with the homeowner in mind, offering models that strike the perfect balance between power and control. Their electric pressure washers deliver consistent pressure suitable for delicate deck surfaces without compromising cleaning performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 2000 PSI enough for cleaning a wooden deck?

Yes, 2000 PSI is ideal for wooden decks. It's powerful enough to remove dirt and algae without damaging the wood surface. Always use a wider spray pattern and maintain proper distance to prevent gouging.

Can I use a pressure washer on pavers or concrete patios?

Absolutely. Concrete and pavers are more durable than wood, so you can safely use 2500-3000 PSI. Giraffe Tools pressure washers with adjustable nozzles make it easy to adapt your cleaning power to different surface types.

How often should I maintain my electric pressure washer?

Electric pressure washers require minimal maintenance. After each use, flush the system with clean water, store it in a dry location, and check hoses for cracks. Winter storage requires running clean water through the system to prevent freezing damage.

What's the difference between hot and cold water pressure washers?

Cold water washers are suitable for most residential deck and patio cleaning. Hot water models are better for grease or oil stains, which are less common on outdoor surfaces. For typical homeowner needs, a quality cold water model like those from Giraffe Tools is sufficient.

Yes, Giraffe Tools stands behind their products with comprehensive warranties. Specific coverage details vary by model, so check their website or product documentation for your chosen pressure washer's warranty terms.
