Frequently Asked Questions

What coverage area should an oscillating sprinkler have?

Most quality oscillating sprinklers cover between 1,200 to 4,000 square feet depending on water pressure. For average residential gardens and raised beds, look for models covering 2,000-3,000 square feet for optimal efficiency.

Can oscillating sprinklers work with low water pressure?

Some models work with water pressure as low as 20 PSI, but most perform best between 40-80 PSI. Check manufacturer specifications before purchasing if you have concerns about your home's water pressure.

How long do oscillating sprinklers typically last?

With proper maintenance and seasonal storage, quality oscillating sprinklers last 3-5 years. Regular cleaning and winter protection extend their lifespan significantly.

Are oscillating sprinklers suitable for vegetable gardens?

Yes, oscillating sprinklers work well for vegetable gardens and can be adjusted to deliver gentle watering patterns that protect delicate seedlings and young plants.
