Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to apply weed control products?

Early morning or late evening applications are ideal, as they minimize stress on desirable plants and allow products to work without intense sun interference. Spring and early summer are optimal seasons for pre-emergent applications, while post-emergent products work best when weeds are actively growing. Elixir Garden Supplies can advise on timing for specific products.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies weed control products safe for vegetable gardens?

Elixir Garden Supplies offers both selective herbicides that can be used around certain vegetables and organic alternatives specifically formulated for edible gardens. Always check product labels and follow application instructions carefully to protect your vegetable crops.

How often should I apply weed control products during summer?

Application frequency depends on the product type and weed pressure in your garden. Pre-emergent applications typically last 4-6 weeks, while post-emergent herbicides may require 7-14 days between applications for persistent weeds. Elixir Garden Supplies product instructions provide specific guidance for optimal results.

Can I combine Elixir Garden Supplies weed control with other garden treatments?

Many Elixir Garden Supplies products can be combined with fertilizers and plant food, but always check compatibility information on product labels. Contact Elixir Garden Supplies customer service for specific combination recommendations.

What should I do if weed control products aren't working?

Persistent weeds may require different approaches. Elixir Garden Supplies offers multiple product types—switching from selective to non-selective herbicides or trying organic alternatives can improve results. Factors like application timing, weather, and weed species all affect effectiveness.
