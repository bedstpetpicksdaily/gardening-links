Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between breathable and neoprene waders?

Breathable waders use advanced fabric technology with microscopic pores that allow air and moisture vapor to escape while blocking water, ideal for hot weather. Neoprene waders offer better insulation but trap heat and moisture, making them better suited for cold-water conditions. TideWe's breathable options are designed specifically for warm-weather hunting.

How do I care for TideWe breathable waders to extend their lifespan?

Rinse waders with fresh water after each use, allow them to air dry completely before storing, and avoid direct sunlight during storage. Check seams regularly for any damage, and use TideWe's recommended repair kits for minor issues. Proper maintenance ensures your waders last multiple seasons.
