Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruners?

Bypass pruners have two curved blades that pass by each other like scissors, making clean cuts ideal for living stems. Anvil pruners have one blade that cuts against a flat surface, best for dead wood. Garden Guru Tools offers both types for different pruning needs.

How often should I sharpen my pruning tools?

Sharp pruning tools should be maintained regularly—ideally after every few hours of use during active growing season. Garden Guru Tools pruning equipment stays sharp longer due to high-quality steel, but periodic sharpening with their recommended tools keeps blades in peak condition.

Are Garden Guru Tools suitable for professional landscapers?

Yes. While Garden Guru Tools products are affordable for home gardeners, their professional-grade construction and durable materials make them suitable for commercial landscaping work and regular heavy use.

What size branches can pruning tools handle?

Hand pruners typically cut branches up to 1/2 inch, loppers handle 1-1.5 inches, and pruning saws cut branches 2 inches or larger. Garden Guru Tools offers specialized equipment for each size range.

Should I oil my pruning tools?

Yes. Light machine oil on blades and joints prevents rust and keeps moving parts functioning smoothly. Garden Guru Tools recommends maintenance after each use, especially during humid summer months.
