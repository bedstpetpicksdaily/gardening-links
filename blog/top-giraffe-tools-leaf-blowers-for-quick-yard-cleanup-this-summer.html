Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Giraffe Tools leaf blower battery last?

Battery runtime varies by model and usage conditions, typically ranging from 20-60 minutes on a single charge. Lighter debris and lower speed settings extend battery life, while heavy-duty use reduces runtime. Multiple batteries allow for continuous work.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for wet leaves?

While electric leaf blowers work best with dry debris, many Giraffe Tools models can handle light moisture. For heavily wet leaves, allowing them to dry slightly or using the blower's higher settings provides better results than attempting to blow dense, damp debris.
