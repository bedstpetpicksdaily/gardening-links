Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start preparing my garden supplies for summer?

Ideally, begin gathering supplies 2-3 weeks before your region's last frost date. This ensures you have everything ready when your plants enter their peak growing season. Early preparation prevents last-minute shortages and allows time to research products like those from Elixir Garden Supplies.

How often should I apply fertilizer during summer?

Most plants benefit from regular feeding every 2-4 weeks during the growing season. However, this varies by plant type and soil conditions. Elixir Garden Supplies provides specific guidance for their products, and it's best to follow recommendations based on your particular plants and garden setup.

What's the best way to store garden chemicals safely?

Store all garden chemicals in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and out of reach of children and pets. Keep products in their original containers with labels intact. A dedicated garden shed or storage cabinet works well. Always follow storage instructions on Elixir Garden Supplies products for optimal safety and effectiveness.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies products organic?

How do I know which fertilizer is right for my plants?

Different plants have different nutritional needs. Vegetables typically need higher nitrogen early on, while flowering plants may benefit from phosphorus and potassium.
