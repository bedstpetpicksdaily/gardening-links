Frequently Asked Questions

When should I apply fertilizers during summer?

Apply slow-release fertilizers in early summer for season-long feeding. Use liquid plant foods every 2-3 weeks for fast-growing plants and heavy feeders like tomatoes and roses. Always follow package instructions and water thoroughly after application.

What's the difference between plant food and fertilizers?

Plant food and fertilizers are often used interchangeably, but plant food typically provides quick nutrient boosts through liquid or water-soluble forms, while fertilizers include granular products that release nutrients slowly. Most gardeners benefit from using both types for comprehensive nutrition.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies products safe for vegetable gardens?

Yes, Elixir Garden Supplies offers specially formulated products suitable for vegetable gardens, including organic-approved options. Always check product labels for harvest timing recommendations and follow application guidelines to ensure safe, healthy produce.

How do weed control products work alongside fertilizers?

Selective herbicides from Elixir target unwanted weeds while protecting desirable plants, eliminating competition for nutrients. When combined with proper fertilization, weeds don't rob your plants of essential nutrition, resulting in healthier, more productive growth.
