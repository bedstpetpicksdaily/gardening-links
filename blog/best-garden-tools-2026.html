FAQ About Garden Tools

What are the 5 essential garden tools every gardener needs?

The five must-have garden tools are a spade or shovel for digging, a hand trowel for planting, pruning shears for cutting branches, a garden hoe for weeding, and a garden hose for watering. These basics cover 90% of typical gardening tasks and work for both beginners and experienced gardeners.

How do I maintain and care for my garden tools?

Clean your tools immediately after use to prevent soil buildup and rust. Store them in a dry location, wipe metal parts with an oily cloth periodically, and sharpen cutting tools annually. Proper maintenance extends tool life and improves performance over time.
