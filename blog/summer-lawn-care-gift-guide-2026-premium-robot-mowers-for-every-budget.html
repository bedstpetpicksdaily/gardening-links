What size lawn is best for a robot mower?

ANTHBOT offers robot mowers suitable for lawns ranging from small urban yards to large residential properties. Most autonomous lawn care systems work best on lawns from 2,000 to 5,000+ square feet, depending on the model. Check ANTHBOT's specifications to find the perfect match for your yard size.

How much maintenance do robotic lawn mowers require?

ANTHBOT's smart lawn mowers require minimal maintenance compared to traditional mowers. Regular blade cleaning, seasonal blade replacement, and charging station maintenance are typical. Most ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers operate autonomously with minimal user intervention once properly set up.

Can robot mowers handle slopes and obstacles?

Yes, ANTHBOT's advanced autonomous lawn care systems are engineered to handle moderate slopes and navigate around obstacles using intelligent sensors and GPS mapping. Their smart lawn mowers automatically detect and avoid garden beds, trees, and hardscaping.

Are ANTHBOT robot mowers safe around pets and children?

ANTHBOT's robotic lawn mowers include safety features and operate at low blade speeds, but supervision is recommended. Schedule automatic grass cutting during times when children and pets are indoors for optimal safety.

What's the battery life of ANTHBOT's smart lawn mowers?

ANTHBOT's robot mowers typically offer 2-4 hours of cutting time per charge, depending on the model and lawn conditions. They automatically return to charging stations and resume work, providing continuous autonomous lawn care throughout the day.
