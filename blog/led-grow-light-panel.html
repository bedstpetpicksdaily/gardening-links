Frequently Asked Questions

What is full spectrum LED lighting for plants?

Full spectrum LED grow lights emit a balanced combination of blue and red wavelengths that replicate natural sunlight, supporting all plant growth stages from germination through flowering and fruiting.

How far should I place an LED grow light panel from my plants?

Most LED grow panels should be placed 12-24 inches above plants, depending on the light's wattage and plant type. Check manufacturer recommendations and adjust height as plants grow.

Can LED grow lights replace natural sunlight?

Yes, full spectrum LED grow lights can effectively replace natural sunlight for indoor gardening, providing the necessary light wavelengths for photosynthesis and healthy plant development.

How many hours should I run my LED grow light daily?

Most indoor plants require 12-16 hours of LED grow light per day. Seedlings and vegetative growth benefit from longer light cycles, while flowering plants may need 12 hours or less.
