Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start fertilizing my summer garden?

Begin fertilizing once plants are actively growing in late spring, then maintain a regular feeding schedule throughout summer. Elixir Garden Supplies offers products suitable for different growth stages and plant types.

How often should I apply weed control products?

Application frequency depends on the product type and weed pressure. Pre-emergent herbicides from Elixir are typically applied once in spring, while post-emergent solutions can be used as needed. Always follow product instructions carefully.

Can I use Elixir Garden Supplies products on vegetable gardens?

Yes, Elixir offers products specifically formulated for vegetable gardens. Check product labels to ensure they're suitable for edible crops and follow application guidelines for safe harvesting.

What's the best way to apply liquid fertilizers?

Liquid fertilizers from Elixir can be applied directly to soil around plant bases or diluted in watering cans for even distribution. Early morning or late evening application minimizes evaporation and ensures better absorption.

How do I choose between granular and liquid plant food?

Liquid feeds provide quick nutrition for fast-growing summer plants, while granular formulas offer slow-release feeding over weeks. Elixir's range includes both options, so choose based on your garden's immediate needs.
