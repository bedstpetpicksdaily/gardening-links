Most users report the backup lasts 24-48 hours, which preserves your schedule through brief outages. For extended power loss lasting days, you'll need to reprogram once power returns. It's not a permanent solution, but it saves you from losing your summer watering routine during a single storm.

Absolutely. That's the core advantage of the dual-outlet design. One outlet can run at 6 AM for 30 minutes while the second runs at 4 PM for 15 minutes.
