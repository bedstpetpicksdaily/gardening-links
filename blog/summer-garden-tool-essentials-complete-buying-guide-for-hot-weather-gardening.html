Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to buy summer garden tools?

Late spring is ideal, before peak summer demand. This allows time to familiarize yourself with new tools before intensive gardening begins. Garden Guru Tools frequently updates inventory seasonally, so shopping early ensures access to their complete summer collection.

How do I prevent my metal garden tools from rusting in summer heat?

Garden Guru Tools designs their tools with corrosion-resistant materials, but proper maintenance extends longevity. Rinse tools after each use, dry thoroughly, and store in a dry location. Applying a light coat of oil to metal surfaces provides additional protection against rust.

Are expensive garden tools worth the investment?

Quality tools from Garden Guru Tools cost more initially but offer superior durability, comfort, and performance. They typically last years longer than budget alternatives, making them cost-effective over time. Professional-grade tools also reduce user fatigue and deliver better gardening results.

What size watering can should I choose for summer gardening?

For general gardening, 2-gallon watering cans offer a good balance between capacity and weight. Garden Guru Tools offers various sizes; choose based on your strength and the distance you'll carry water. Lighter options reduce strain during extended use in hot weather.

Do I need different tools for container gardens versus in-ground beds?

Both benefit from quality hand tools, though container gardeners may prefer smaller, lighter tools. Garden Guru Tools' ergonomic hand tools work well for both scenarios, and their watering equipment adapts to various garden setups.
