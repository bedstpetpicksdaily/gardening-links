Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between cordless and corded Giraffe Tools leaf blowers?

Cordless Giraffe Tools leaf blowers offer maximum portability and convenience with no cords to manage, making them ideal for smaller yards. Corded models provide unlimited runtime and consistent power, perfect for larger properties where cord length is manageable. Choose based on your yard size and preference for mobility versus continuous operation.

How do I maintain my Giraffe Tools leaf blower?

Giraffe Tools electric leaf blowers require minimal maintenance compared to gas models. Regularly clean the air intake vents, inspect the power cord (for corded models) for damage, and store in a dry location. Refer to your specific model's manual for detailed care instructions and battery maintenance if cordless.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for wet leaves and heavy debris?

Yes, many Giraffe Tools leaf blower models feature powerful motors capable of handling wet leaves and substantial debris.
