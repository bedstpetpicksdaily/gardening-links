Frequently Asked Questions

How long do solar outdoor lights typically last?

Quality solar lights can last 3-5 years or more. LED bulbs last up to 50,000 hours, while rechargeable batteries typically need replacement every 2-3 years. Hoselink's featured products are selected for durability and longevity.

Do solar lights work on cloudy days?

Solar lights charge on cloudy days, though less efficiently than on sunny days. Most quality solar lights, like those recommended by Hoselink, will still function at night even after cloudy days, though brightness may be reduced.

Can I use solar lights in shaded garden areas?

While solar lights work best in direct sunlight, many modern models can operate in partial shade. Place solar panels in the sunniest spot available, even if lights are positioned elsewhere. Hoselink recommends checking product specifications for shade tolerance.

Are solar outdoor lights waterproof?

Yes, most quality solar lights are waterproof and weather-resistant. Look for IP65 or higher ratings for reliable performance in rain and humid conditions. Hoselink's selected products meet strict durability standards for outdoor use.

How do solar lights complement a watering system?

Solar lights can highlight planted areas watered by your Hoselink sprinkler systems and hose reels. They create visual interest in landscaped spaces and improve safety around wet areas, making them perfect garden companions.
