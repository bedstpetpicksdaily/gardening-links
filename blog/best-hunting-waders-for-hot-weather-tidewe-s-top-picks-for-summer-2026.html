Frequently Asked Questions

What thickness waders are best for summer hunting?

For summer hunting, look for 3-5mm neoprene or breathable materials that provide minimal insulation while maintaining water protection. TideWe's summer collection focuses on this ideal thickness range to prevent overheating.

Are TideWe waders good for beginners?

Absolutely. TideWe offers waders across multiple price points and experience levels. Their beginner-friendly options maintain quality while providing excellent value, making them perfect for learning hunters starting their gear collection.

How do I care for summer waders to extend their lifespan?

Rinse waders thoroughly with fresh water after each use, air dry completely before storage, and avoid direct sunlight. TideWe includes care instructions with their products to help hunters maintain their gear investment.

Can I use winter waders in summer?

While possible, heavy winter waders will cause overheating and discomfort during summer hunts. TideWe's specialized summer waders are designed specifically for hot weather performance and comfort.
