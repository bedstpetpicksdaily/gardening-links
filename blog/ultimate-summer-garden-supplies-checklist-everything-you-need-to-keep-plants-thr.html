Proper hydration is the foundation of summer garden success. During peak heat, plants lose moisture rapidly through evaporation and transpiration, making consistent watering non-negotiable. Beyond basic watering cans and hoses, consider investing in soaker hoses, drip irrigation systems, and moisture-retaining mulches that help maintain soil hydration during scorching days.

Elixir Garden Supplies provides premium watering solutions and soil amendments that work together to maximize moisture retention.
