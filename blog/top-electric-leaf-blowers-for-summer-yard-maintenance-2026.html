Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical electric leaf blower battery last?

Modern lithium-ion batteries in quality models like Giraffe Tools typically provide 30-60 minutes of runtime per charge, depending on speed setting and debris load. Most homeowners find one battery sufficient for average residential lots, with the option to purchase spares for extended projects.

Are electric leaf blowers powerful enough for heavy debris?

Yes, contemporary electric leaf blowers deliver airflow comparable to gas models. Giraffe Tools models produce sufficient CFM (cubic feet per minute) to handle leaves, grass clippings, and light debris. Variable speed settings let you optimize power for different tasks.

Can I use Giraffe Tools batteries across multiple devices?

Yes, Giraffe Tools designs their battery ecosystem for compatibility across various outdoor power equipment. This allows you to invest strategically in their pressure washers, snow blowers, and other tools while sharing batteries—increasing overall value and reducing costs.

What's the warranty on electric leaf blowers?

Giraffe Tools typically offers comprehensive warranties on their equipment. Check their official website or contact customer service for specific coverage details on your chosen model.

How do I maintain my electric leaf blower?

Electric leaf blowers require minimal maintenance. Keep air intake vents clean, store batteries in cool conditions, and inspect the blower housing for damage occasionally. Unlike gas models, there's no fuel system to maintain or seasonal winterization needed.
