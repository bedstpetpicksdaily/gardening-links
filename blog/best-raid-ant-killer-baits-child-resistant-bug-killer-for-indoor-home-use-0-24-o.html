Frequently Asked Questions

Is Raid ant killer safe to use around children and pets?

Raid ant killer baits with child-resistant packaging are designed to prevent accidental ingestion, but should still be placed in areas inaccessible to children and pets. Always follow the label instructions carefully and keep the product in its original container away from play areas and pet food bowls.

How long does it take for Raid ant baits to kill the colony?

Raid ant baits typically begin showing results within 24-48 hours as worker ants carry the poison back to the nest, with full colony elimination usually occurring within 7-14 days depending on colony size. For best results, do not clean up dead ants or disturb bait stations during this period.

What is the 0.24% ingredient in Raid ant killer baits?

The 0.24% active ingredient refers to the concentration of the pesticide (commonly fipronil or hydramethylnon) that targets the ant's nervous system. This low percentage is formulated to be lethal to ants while minimizing toxicity when used as directed in homes.

Can I use Raid ant baits indoors throughout my entire house?

Yes, Raid ant baits are specifically formulated for indoor home use and can be placed along baseboards, under sinks, and in kitchen cabinets where ants are active. Place baits in corners and along ant trails rather than in high-traffic areas where children or pets might disturb them.
