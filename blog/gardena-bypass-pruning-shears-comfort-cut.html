Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum cutting diameter for Gardena Bypass Pruning Shears?

These shears are designed to cut branches and stems up to approximately 3/4 inch in diameter. For thicker branches, consider using a lopper or pruning saw instead.

How do I maintain and sharpen my Gardena pruning shears?

Keep blades clean after each use and wipe dry. Sharpen the bypass blade annually with a whetstone or sharpening tool. Apply light oil to pivot points to prevent rust and ensure smooth operation.

Are Gardena Comfort Cut shears suitable for left-handed gardeners?

While these shears feature an ergonomic handle, check the product specifications for left-handed versions, as some Gardena models offer both right and left-handed options.

Can I use these shears for cutting flowers and fresh produce?

Yes, the bypass blade design makes them excellent for cutting flowers, herbs, and vegetables without damaging delicate stems, making them versatile for both garden maintenance and harvest tasks.
