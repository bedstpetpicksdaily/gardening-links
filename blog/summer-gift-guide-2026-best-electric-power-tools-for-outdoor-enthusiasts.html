How long do electric tool batteries last?

Modern lithium-ion batteries used by Giraffe Tools typically retain 80% capacity after 500-1000 charge cycles, translating to 3-5 years of regular use. Most models charge fully in 30-90 minutes depending on battery size.

Yes, modern electric pressure washers like those from Giraffe Tools can deliver comparable PSI ratings (typically 2000-3000 PSI) to entry and mid-level gas models, with the added benefits of quieter operation and easier maintenance.
