Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up a robotic lawn mower?

Initial setup typically takes 2-3 hours and involves installing boundary wire, configuring the base station, and programming your desired cutting schedule through the mobile app. After setup, the system operates autonomously with minimal maintenance.

Can robot mowers handle slopes and uneven terrain?

Quality robotic mowers like those from ANTHBOT can navigate slopes up to 35-40 degrees. Advanced sensors detect terrain changes and adjust cutting height and speed accordingly for optimal performance on varied landscapes.

How often does a robotic lawn mower need maintenance?

Minimal maintenance is required. Monthly blade inspections, occasional blade replacements (every 6-8 months depending on use), and keeping the charging contacts clean are the primary tasks. ANTHBOT's durability design reduces maintenance needs significantly.

Are robotic lawn mowers safe around pets and children?

Yes, modern robot mowers feature multiple safety sensors that detect obstacles and stop immediately. However, supervision is recommended for very young children, and boundary wires should keep the mower away from pet play areas.

What happens if a robotic mower breaks down during the season?

Most manufacturers, including ANTHBOT, offer comprehensive warranty coverage and customer support. Professional repair services are available, and loaner mowers are often provided to minimize lawn care disruptions.
