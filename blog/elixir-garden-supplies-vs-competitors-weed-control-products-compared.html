Frequently Asked Questions

Is Elixir Garden Supplies weed control safe for pets and children?

Elixir products should be applied according to label instructions and kept away from pets and children during application. Once dry, most formulations are safe, but always follow specific product guidelines and store safely away from household members.

How long do Elixir weed control products take to work?

Most Elixir weed killers show visible results within 7-14 days, depending on the product type and weed species. Contact herbicides work faster (3-7 days), while systemic formulas take longer but provide more complete root elimination.

Elixir offers different products for different applications—selective herbicides for lawns, total weed killers for patios and driveways, and moss control for various surfaces. Always check the product label to ensure it's suitable for your specific area.
