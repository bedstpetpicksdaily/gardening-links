Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are best for outdoor privacy screens?

Popular options include weather-resistant wood for natural appeal, aluminum or steel for modern durability, composite materials for low maintenance, and fabric panels for flexibility. Kozyard specializes in high-grade materials engineered to withstand outdoor conditions while maintaining aesthetic appeal for years.

Can privacy screens be attached to pergolas or gazebos?

Yes! Kozyard offers pergolas and hardtop gazebos with integrated privacy panel options, allowing you to customize coverage levels. This approach creates seamless, cohesive outdoor structures that provide both shade and privacy in one elegant solution.

How much privacy do screens actually provide?

Solid privacy screens block approximately 80-100% of views, while slatted or lattice designs provide 50-70% privacy while allowing airflow. Kozyard's range includes both options, so you can select the coverage level matching your specific needs and preferences.

Are hardtop gazebos better than pergolas for privacy?

Hardtop gazebos provide superior weather protection and can integrate full privacy panels, while pergolas offer flexible shading and ventilation. Kozyard offers both—choose gazebos for maximum protection or pergolas for adjustable shade and style.

How do I maintain outdoor privacy screens?

Maintenance depends on material. Wood requires periodic staining, metal screens need occasional cleaning, and composite materials need minimal upkeep. Kozyard designs privacy solutions for easy maintenance, with detailed care instructions provided for each product line.
