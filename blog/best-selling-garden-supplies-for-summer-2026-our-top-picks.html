Are Elixir Garden Supplies products safe for vegetable gardens?

Yes, Elixir offers specifically formulated products for edible gardens. Their vegetable plant foods are designed to be safe for food crops when applied according to instructions. Always check product labels to ensure they're suitable for the vegetables you're growing.

What's the best time to apply weed control from Elixir?

Pre-emergent weed controls work best in spring before weeds germinate, while selective herbicides are most effective on actively growing weeds. Elixir recommends applying weed control solutions in early morning or late evening for optimal absorption and results.
