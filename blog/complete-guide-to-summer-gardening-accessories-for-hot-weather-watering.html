Summer heat intensifies water evaporation, creating unique demands on garden watering systems. Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my garden during summer?

Most plants need watering once or twice daily during extreme heat, but this depends on soil type, plant species, and rainfall. Garden Guru Tools' moisture sensors take the guesswork out by monitoring actual soil moisture levels.

What's the best time to water a summer garden?

Early morning (before 8 AM) is ideal because it reduces evaporation and allows plants to absorb water before peak heat. Programmable watering timers from Garden Guru Tools automate this schedule perfectly.

Are drip irrigation systems worth the investment?

Yes. Drip systems from Garden Guru Tools reduce water waste by 30-40%, lower your water bills, and deliver superior plant health by providing consistent soil moisture directly at the root zone.
