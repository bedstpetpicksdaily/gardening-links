Will this clog if I have hard water?

Probably yes, eventually. The included filter catches larger particles, but mineral deposits build up in emitters over time with hard water (calcium/magnesium deposits). Users with hard water report cleaning emitters every 2-3 weeks during peak growing season. Install an inline water softener or use collected rainwater if possible. If your tap water is already soft, clogging is minimal and you'll maintain the system maybe once a month.
