What's the difference between concentrated and ready-to-use fertilizers?

Concentrated fertilizers require dilution with water, meaning smaller bottles deliver more applications—making them cost-effective. Ready-to-use products come prepared but cost more per application. Elixir offers both types; concentrated options typically provide better value during summer sales for bulk-purchasing gardeners.

How should I store garden chemicals purchased during summer sales?
