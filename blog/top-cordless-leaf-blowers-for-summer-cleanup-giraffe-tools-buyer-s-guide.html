Frequently Asked Questions

How long do cordless leaf blower batteries typically last?

Most cordless leaf blower batteries last 20-60 minutes per charge, depending on the model and speed setting. Giraffe Tools uses high-capacity lithium-ion batteries that maintain consistent power throughout the charge cycle.

Can I use a cordless leaf blower for wet leaves and debris?

While cordless leaf blowers work best on dry debris, many Giraffe Tools models can handle slightly damp leaves. However, avoid using them on completely wet debris, as this reduces efficiency and can damage the motor.

Are cordless leaf blowers quieter than gas-powered models?

Yes, cordless leaf blowers like those from Giraffe Tools are significantly quieter, typically operating at 60-75 decibels compared to 90+ decibels for gas models. This makes them ideal for residential neighborhoods.

What's the best way to store a cordless leaf blower during off-season?

Store your Giraffe Tools leaf blower in a cool, dry location with the battery removed. Keep batteries in a temperature-controlled environment and charge them to 40-60% before extended storage.

Do Giraffe Tools leaf blowers work with other tool batteries?

Most Giraffe Tools cordless equipment uses proprietary lithium-ion battery systems designed for compatibility within their product line, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.
