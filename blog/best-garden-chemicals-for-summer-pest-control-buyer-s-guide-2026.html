Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to apply garden pest control chemicals?

Early morning or late evening applications work best, as pests are most active and beneficial insects are less likely to be foraging. Always check product instructions for optimal application windows and weather conditions.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies chemicals safe for organic gardens?

Elixir offers both organic-certified and synthetic options. Their organic range includes neem oil, pyrethrin, and other naturally-derived solutions suitable for organic gardening practices.

How often should I apply pest control chemicals during summer?

Most garden chemicals require application every 7-14 days depending on pest pressure and product type. Always follow manufacturer recommendations on the label for best results and safety.

Can I combine multiple pest control chemicals?

Some products can be tank-mixed, but many cannot. Always consult product labels and Elixir's guidance before combining chemicals to avoid inefficacy or phytotoxicity.

What should I do if pests become resistant to chemicals?

Rotate between different chemical classes and consider integrated pest management approaches. Elixir Garden Supplies staff can recommend rotation schedules to prevent resistance development.
