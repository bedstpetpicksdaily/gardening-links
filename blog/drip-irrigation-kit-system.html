Frequently Asked Questions

What size drip irrigation kit do I need for my raised garden bed?

Measure your garden bed's length and width, then add 20% extra for branch lines. Most kits cover 100-500 square feet. Starter kits work well for beds under 100 sq ft, while larger gardens benefit from expandable systems.

Can I use a drip irrigation kit with container plants and planters?

Yes, drip systems work excellently for containers. Use adjustable emitters or drip lines designed for pots and planters. You can customize water flow for different plant sizes and water needs.

Do I need a timer for my drip irrigation system?

Timers aren't required but highly recommended. Battery or solar-powered timers provide automatic watering on your schedule, perfect for vacation watering and consistent plant health.

How often should I maintain my drip irrigation kit?

Check and clean filters monthly, flush lines quarterly, and inspect emitters for clogs. At season's end, drain the system completely and store in a cool, dry place.
