What size waders should I choose from TideWe?

TideWe offers sizing charts for all wader styles. Measure your height, weight, and chest/waist circumference carefully. Most customers find chest waders work best for fishing and water hunting, while waist waders suit shallower water activities. Contact TideWe customer service if you're between sizes.

Are TideWe boots insulated for winter use?

TideWe offers both insulated and non-insulated boot options. Their insulated models feature thermal lining suitable for cold-weather hunting. For summer use, non-insulated boots provide better breathability while still offering waterproof protection and support.

How do I care for and maintain my TideWe waders?

Rinse your TideWe waders with fresh water after each use, dry them thoroughly in a cool location away from direct sunlight, and store them flat or hung to prevent creasing. Avoid harsh detergents and apply waterproofing treatments annually for optimal performance.

Absolutely. TideWe's waterproof boots, aprons, and work accessories are designed for both hunting and yard work. Their durable materials protect against moisture, mud, and rough surfaces, making them ideal for serious outdoor projects beyond hunting.
