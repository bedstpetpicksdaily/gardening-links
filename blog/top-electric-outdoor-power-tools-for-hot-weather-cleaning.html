Frequently Asked Questions

Are electric pressure washers powerful enough for heavy-duty cleaning?

Yes, modern electric models like those from Giraffe Tools deliver 1500-3000+ PSI, sufficient for driveways, patios, decks, and siding. They're ideal for most residential cleaning needs and eliminate the maintenance hassles of gas versions.

How long do batteries last on cordless electric tools?

Battery runtime varies by model and usage intensity, typically ranging from 20-90 minutes per charge. For larger properties, purchasing an extra battery ensures uninterrupted cleaning sessions.

Can electric tools handle continuous hot weather use?

Electric tools are actually superior in hot conditions—they produce no fumes, generate minimal heat, and reduce operator fatigue. Giraffe Tools designs specifically account for extended summer use with efficient motors and cooling systems.

What maintenance do electric outdoor power tools require?

Electric tools require minimal maintenance: rinse and dry after use, store batteries properly, and occasionally inspect hoses and nozzles. No oil, spark plugs, or seasonal servicing needed—just plug in and clean.
