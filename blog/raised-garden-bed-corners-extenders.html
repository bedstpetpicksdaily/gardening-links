What to Look For

Look for galvanized steel, powder-coated metal, or high-grade composite materials that resist rust and UV damage. Cheap plastic corners crack within 1-2 seasons; quality materials last 5-10+ years. Check reviews specifically mentioning how corners held up after multiple seasons of use.

Measure your current bed's board thickness and height before purchasing. Most extenders are designed for standard 2x6 or 2x8 boards, but some work with 2x10 or 2x12 boards. Verify the corner system fits your specific board dimensions—incompatibility is the #1 reason for returns.

The best corners and extenders require no tools or minimal hardware—look for bolt-together systems or screwless interlocking designs. Avoid options requiring welding, drilling, or extensive fastening. Installation should take under 30 minutes for most projects.
