Frequently Asked Questions

How long do soaker hoses typically last?

Quality soaker hoses with UV-resistant materials can last 3-5 years with proper storage and maintenance. Storing them indoors during winter and protecting them from direct sunlight extends their lifespan significantly.

Can I connect multiple soaker hoses together?

Yes, most soaker hoses feature connector fittings that allow you to join multiple hoses together for larger garden coverage. Ensure connections are tight to prevent leaks.

How much water does a soaker hose use?

Soaker hoses typically use 0.5-1 gallon per foot per hour, making them 50% more efficient than traditional sprinklers. This varies based on water pressure and hose design.

Are soaker hoses suitable for all plant types?

Soaker hoses work well for most plants including vegetables, shrubs, and ornamentals. However, they're especially beneficial for water-sensitive plants and drought-resistant gardens.
