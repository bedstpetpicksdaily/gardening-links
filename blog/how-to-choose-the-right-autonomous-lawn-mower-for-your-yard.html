Many traditional robot mowers require boundary wires, but ANTHBOT's advanced models use GPS and smart mapping technology, eliminating the need for extensive installation. Some models may have optional perimeter setup for enhanced accuracy.

Robot mowers require minimal maintenance compared to gas-powered alternatives. Regular tasks include cleaning the deck, sharpening blades seasonally, and keeping the charging dock clear. ANTHBOT's designs prioritize easy maintenance to keep your mower operating smoothly.
