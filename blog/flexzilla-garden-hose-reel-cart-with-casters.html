Frequently Asked Questions

Will this cart fit through standard garden gates?

Most standard gates (36-42 inches wide) will work, but measure first. The cart itself is roughly 30 inches wide without the handle, so there's modest clearance. Narrower cottage gates or ornamental metal gates might be tight—I had to angle mine through my side gate, which was slightly annoying but manageable.

Can the Flexzilla hose work with standard brass fittings?

Yes, completely. The Flexzilla hose uses universal connector sizes. Attach it to your standard outdoor faucet, connect your favorite spray nozzle—no compatibility issues. The advantage is the hose itself won't kink like older rubber hoses, so fittings last longer because they're not being twisted repeatedly.

How does this perform in direct sun during summer?

The hose material actually holds up well in July heat without becoming brittle or cracking. Water inside gets warm, naturally, but the hose doesn't degrade. The wheels can get hot to touch on dark mulch, but that's minor. One note: if you leave it sitting in blazing sun for hours, the water inside will get genuinely hot, so use caution when first opening the spray nozzle—expect hot water initially.
