Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between lightweight and traditional waders?

Lightweight waders use breathable synthetic or thin neoprene materials that reduce weight and bulk while maintaining waterproofing. Traditional waders use thicker materials for extreme cold protection. TideWe's lightweight options prioritize breathability and comfort for warm weather without sacrificing durability.

Are TideWe lightweight waders suitable for all hunting types?

TideWe lightweight waders excel in warm-weather waterfowl hunting, marsh hunting, and summer upland pursuits. They're less ideal for winter hunting in freezing conditions. Consider your specific climate and season when selecting from TideWe's wader lineup.

How do I care for my TideWe lightweight waders?

Rinse waders with fresh water after each use, allow them to air dry completely, and store in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight. TideWe provides detailed care instructions with each purchase to maximize wader lifespan.
