Are TideWe lightweight waders suitable for saltwater hunting?

How do I care for lightweight breathable waders?

Clean waders after each use, dry completely before storage, and store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight. Avoid folding in the same spots repeatedly to prevent creasing.
