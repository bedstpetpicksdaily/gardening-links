Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Traverseon coolers keep ice frozen?

Traverseon coolers can maintain ice for 7-10 days under optimal conditions with proper packing techniques. Actual duration depends on ambient temperature, cooler size, and how frequently the lid is opened.

What size cooler do I need for a week-long camping trip?

For extended trips, the rule of thumb is 1.5 to 2 pounds of ice per person per day. A 70-90 quart cooler typically handles a week for a family of 4-5, though this varies based on meal planning and local store access.

Are Traverseon coolers worth the premium price?

Yes, for extended trips and frequent camping. Superior insulation means better ice retention, reducing food waste and replacement ice purchases. The durability also ensures your cooler lasts for years of adventures.

Can I use a camping cooler for RV travel?

Absolutely. Many campers use high-capacity coolers as backup refrigeration for RV trips. Traverseon coolers are popular RV accessories for supplemental food storage and keeping backup supplies cold.
