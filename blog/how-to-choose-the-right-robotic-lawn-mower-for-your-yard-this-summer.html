Assess Your Yard Size and Terrain

The first step in choosing a robotic lawn mower is understanding your yard's dimensions and topography. Robot mowers come in various sizes, each designed to handle different lawn areas efficiently. Small yards under 2,500 square feet require a compact mower, while larger properties may need a more powerful model. Additionally, consider your terrain—do you have hills, slopes, or uneven ground? ANTHBOT's range of smart lawn mowers is engineered to handle diverse yard conditions, with models specifically designed for both flat and sloped terrain, ensuring optimal performance regardless of your landscape.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to install a robotic lawn mower?

Most robotic lawn mowers, including ANTHBOT models, require 2-4 hours for initial setup. This involves installing boundary wire around your lawn's perimeter and charging the unit. Once configured, the system operates autonomously with minimal ongoing maintenance.

Can robotic lawn mowers handle wet grass?

ANTHBOT's smart lawn mowers are designed to work in various weather conditions, though most perform best on dry to slightly damp grass. Their weather sensors automatically adjust schedules during heavy rain, ensuring optimal cutting conditions and protecting your lawn.

Do I need to remove obstacles from my yard?

While ANTHBOT's autonomous lawn care systems include obstacle detection, it's best practice to remove small toys, garden tools, and debris before operation. This ensures safe, efficient automatic grass cutting and protects both your belongings and the mower.

How often should I maintain my robotic lawn mower?

ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers require minimal maintenance—primarily blade inspection every 2-4 weeks and occasional cleaning. Regular maintenance ensures consistent performance and extends your mower's lifespan throughout multiple seasons.
