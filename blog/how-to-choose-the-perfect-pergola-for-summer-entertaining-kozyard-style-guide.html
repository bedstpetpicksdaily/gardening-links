Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a pergola and a hardtop gazebo?

Pergolas feature an open slatted roof design that provides partial shade and allows airflow, while hardtop gazebos from Kozyard offer complete protection with solid roofs. Choose a pergola for an airy, open feel or a hardtop gazebo for maximum weather protection during entertaining.

How much shade does a Kozyard pergola provide?

Kozyard pergolas provide dappled shade through their slatted roof design, offering sun protection without creating a completely enclosed space. For maximum shade, consider their hardtop gazebos or add Kozyard's compatible awnings and privacy screens for adjustable coverage.

Are Kozyard pergolas easy to maintain?

Yes, Kozyard's aluminum pergolas are engineered for low maintenance. Unlike wood structures, aluminum resists rot, rust, and pest damage, requiring only occasional cleaning to maintain their appearance and functionality.

Can I customize my Kozyard pergola for entertainment?

Absolutely. Kozyard offers various sizes, colors, and compatible accessories including lighting, planters, privacy screens, and awnings that allow you to customize your pergola space to match your entertaining style and needs.

What's the best way to accessorize a pergola for entertaining?

Pair your Kozyard pergola with their patio furniture, planters, and privacy screens to create a complete outdoor room. Add string lights or integrated lighting options for ambiance, and consider weather-resistant textiles for comfort and style.
