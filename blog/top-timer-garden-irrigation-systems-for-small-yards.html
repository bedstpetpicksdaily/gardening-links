Choose systems with timers that allow you to set multiple start times per day in 5-15 minute increments, giving you precise control without overwatering delicate seedlings or young transplants.
  • Simple Setup and Adjustability: Since small yards often have irregular shapes and mixed planting types, your system needs to adapt easily.
