Frequently Asked Questions

Do seed starting kits with grow lights work for all plant types?

Yes, most full-spectrum LED grow lights support vegetables, herbs, flowers, and leafy greens. However, check light intensity (measured in lumens) to ensure it meets requirements for your specific plants.

How many hours per day should grow lights run for seedlings?

Seedlings typically need 14-16 hours of light daily. Many kits include automatic timers to maintain consistent schedules, which promotes strong, compact growth.

What's the difference between LED and fluorescent grow lights?

LED lights are more energy-efficient, produce less heat, last longer, and provide better full-spectrum light. Fluorescent options are cheaper but consume more electricity and require more frequent replacement.

Can I adjust the height of grow lights as seedlings grow?

Yes, most quality kits feature adjustable light fixtures on rails or chains. Keeping lights 2-3 inches above seedlings prevents stretching and promotes compact, healthy growth.
