Frequently Asked Questions

What herbs typically come in a starter kit?

Most herb garden starter kits include popular culinary herbs such as basil, parsley, cilantro, chives, oregano, and thyme. Some premium kits may include additional varieties like mint or rosemary.

How long does it take to grow herbs from a starter kit?

Most herbs are ready to harvest within 3-6 weeks of planting, depending on the herb variety and growing conditions. Basil and parsley typically grow faster than slower-growing herbs like oregano.

Can I use an herb garden starter kit indoors?

Yes, many starter kits are designed for indoor growing near a sunny window. Some kits include grow lights for optimal indoor growing, while others work best with natural sunlight.

Do I need gardening experience to use a starter kit?

No, herb garden starter kits are specifically designed for beginners. They include detailed instructions and all necessary supplies, making them ideal for anyone new to gardening.
