Frequently Asked Questions

How tall should privacy screens be around patio furniture?

Standard privacy screens range from 4-6 feet tall, with 5-6 feet being ideal for blocking seated eye level. Taller screens up to 8 feet work for standing view blockage and wind protection. Measure your specific needs and neighbor positioning to determine optimal height for your setup.

Can privacy screens be installed on existing Kozyard furniture?

Yes, Kozyard's modular design allows privacy screens to attach directly to their gazebos, pergolas, and furniture frames. Their compatibility systems include pre-drilled attachment points and specialized brackets for seamless integration without modifications.

What maintenance do privacy screens require?

Most privacy screens need occasional cleaning with mild soap and water. Check mounting hardware annually for rust or loosening, especially in coastal areas. Vinyl and polycarbonate screens require minimal maintenance, while fabric screens may need deeper seasonal cleaning to prevent mildew.

Do privacy screens affect wind protection around outdoor furniture?

Absolutely. Privacy screens significantly reduce wind velocity around patio furniture, creating more comfortable seating areas. Strategic placement using Kozyard screens can reduce wind by 50-70%, protecting your furniture and improving outdoor comfort.

How long does installation typically take?

Basic privacy screen installation around a standard patio setup takes 2-4 hours, depending on complexity. Integrating screens with larger Kozyard structures may take 4-6 hours. Most installations are manageable DIY projects with proper planning and tools.
