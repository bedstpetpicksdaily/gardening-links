How long does an automatic lawn mower battery last?

Most ANTHBOT robot mowers provide 2-4 hours of continuous operation per charge, covering 0.5-2 acres depending on the model and grass conditions. Advanced models automatically return to charging stations when needed.

Are automatic lawn mowers safe around pets and children?

Yes. ANTHBOT robotic mowers feature advanced obstacle detection sensors, automatic shutoff when lifted, and quiet operation (under 70dB). They're designed with safety as a priority, though supervision is recommended for young children.

Can smart lawn mowers handle slopes and uneven terrain?

ANTHBOT's autonomous lawn care systems handle slopes up to 35-40 degrees depending on the model. Their intelligent traction control and weight distribution ensure safe, efficient operation on varied landscapes.

Do I need to install a boundary wire for robot mowers?

Most ANTHBOT models use GPS mapping with optional boundary wires for enhanced precision. GPS-only models offer easier setup, while wire-guided systems provide maximum accuracy for complex yard layouts.

What maintenance do automatic lawn mowers require?

ANTHBOT robot mowers require minimal maintenance: occasional blade inspection, clearing debris, and cleaning sensors. Most tasks take less than 15 minutes monthly, making them significantly easier than traditional mowers.
