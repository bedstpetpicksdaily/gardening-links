1. Weather Intelligence & Real-Time Adjustment

The best smart watering timers skip watering when rain is forecasted or has recently fallen. Look for systems that automatically adjust schedules based on local weather data, temperature changes, and humidity levels. Most smart timers control 2-4 zones independently, allowing you to water your drought-tolerant succulent bed less frequently than your thirsty vegetable garden.
