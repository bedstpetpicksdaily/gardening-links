Frequently Asked Questions

Are electric pressure washers as powerful as gas models?

Modern electric pressure washers, especially Giraffe Tools models, deliver comparable power for residential cleaning tasks. While some industrial gas models may exceed 4000 PSI, most homeowners find electric options with 2000-3000 PSI more than sufficient for driveways, patios, and home exteriors.

How long does a Giraffe Tools electric pressure washer last?

Giraffe Tools electric pressure washers are built for longevity with premium brushless motors and durable construction. With proper maintenance and regular use, most models last 5-10+ years, significantly outlasting competitors in the budget segment.

Can I use an electric pressure washer in rainy conditions?

While Giraffe Tools pressure washers feature water-resistant designs, using them during heavy rain isn't recommended. Light drizzle is generally safe, but always ensure your power source (outlet or extension cord) has proper GFCI protection for safety.

What PSI do I need for general home cleaning?

For most residential tasks like cleaning decks, patios, and siding, 1500-2500 PSI is ideal. Giraffe Tools' versatile pressure washers offer adjustable settings, allowing you to reduce pressure for delicate surfaces and increase it for stubborn stains.
