How long does Clorox Pool Spa Shock Xtrablue Plus take to kill algae and bacteria?

Clorox Pool Spa Shock Xtrablue Plus typically begins killing algae and bacteria within 24 hours of application, with most visible results appearing within 48 hours. How much Clorox Pool Spa Shock Xtrablue Plus do I need for my pool size?

Standard dosing is 1 pound per 10,000 gallons of pool water for routine shocking, though heavily contaminated pools may require double-dosing. Always calculate your exact pool volume first and follow the label instructions, as using too little won't be effective and too much can damage equipment.

Can I swim immediately after adding Clorox Pool Spa Shock Xtrablue Plus?

You should wait at least 8-24 hours after application before swimming, depending on your pool's chlorine levels and conditions. Test your chlorine levels with a pool kit to ensure they're in the safe range (2-4 ppm) before allowing swimmers back in.

Does Clorox Pool Spa Shock Xtrablue Plus turn the water blue?

Xtrablue Plus contains a blue clarifier dye that helps you see the shock spreading through your pool and doesn't permanently tint the water blue. The color dissipates as the product works, and your pool returns to normal clarity once treatment is complete.
