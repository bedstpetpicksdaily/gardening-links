Frequently Asked Questions

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for wet leaves?

Yes, Giraffe Tools leaf blowers can handle damp leaves, though they perform best on dry debris. For wet leaves, use the maximum speed setting. Always ensure proper ventilation of the intake area to prevent clogging.

How long does the battery last on Giraffe Tools cordless leaf blowers?

Battery runtime varies by model and debris type, typically ranging from 30 to 60 minutes on a single charge. Lighter cleanup tasks use less battery than maximum power operation, extending your working time.

Can I use Giraffe Tools leaf blower batteries with other products?

Many Giraffe Tools batteries are compatible across their electric outdoor power tools lineup, including pressure washers and snow blowers. Check your specific model to confirm compatibility with other Giraffe Tools equipment.

What maintenance do Giraffe Tools electric leaf blowers require?

Electric leaf blowers require minimal maintenance—no spark plugs, oil changes, or fuel mixing. Simply keep the intake and exhaust vents clean, store in a dry location, and follow battery care recommendations for optimal performance.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers quieter than gas models?

Yes, Giraffe Tools electric leaf blowers operate significantly quieter than gas-powered alternatives, typically producing 80-90 decibels compared to 100+ decibels for gas blowers, making them neighbor-friendly.
