Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a robotic lawn mower and a traditional mower?

Robotic lawn mowers operate autonomously using GPS navigation and smart scheduling, requiring minimal human intervention. ANTHBOT's automatic grass cutting systems work on set schedules, while traditional mowers require manual operation. Robotic models are quieter, more eco-friendly, and save significant time.

Can ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers handle slopes and uneven terrain?

Most ANTHBOT models handle slopes up to 35% gradient effectively. However, robotic lawn mowers perform best on relatively even terrain. Steep hills, dense obstacles, or extremely uneven ground may require manual zones or periodic touch-ups.
