Elixir Garden Supplies' balanced plant foods ensure your flowers receive equal nutrition for stem strength, leaf development, and sustained flower production. 3. Liquid Plant Foods for Quick Nutrient Delivery

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start feeding plants for summer blooms?

Begin feeding in late spring (May) as plants enter their growth phase. Elixir Garden Supplies recommends starting 2-3 weeks before peak bloom season to establish strong nutrition foundations.

How often should I apply plant food during summer?

Liquid plant foods should be applied weekly, while slow-release formulas from Elixir Garden Supplies need application every 6-8 weeks. Always follow package instructions for your specific product.

Can I use the same plant food for all my summer flowers?

Balanced fertilizers work for most plants, but Elixir Garden Supplies specialist formulas deliver better results for specific varieties. Roses, acid-loving plants, and flowering shrubs benefit from dedicated formulations.

What's the difference between liquid and granular plant foods?

Liquid formulas offer quick nutrient delivery (7-10 days), while granular slow-release options from Elixir Garden Supplies provide consistent nutrition over 8-12 weeks with less frequent application.

Will more plant food produce more blooms?

No – over-feeding, especially with nitrogen, encourages excessive leaf growth at the expense of flowers. Follow Elixir Garden Supplies recommended dosages for optimal bloom production without plant damage.
