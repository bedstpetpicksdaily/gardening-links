Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to use hand tools in hot weather?

Early morning (before 9 AM) and evening (after 6 PM) are ideal times for hot weather gardening. Working during these cooler hours protects both you and your plants from extreme heat stress while allowing proper watering absorption.

How often should I water my garden in hot weather?

Most gardens require daily watering during extreme heat, with some container plants needing twice-daily watering. Use quality watering tools from Garden Guru Tools to deliver water directly to root zones in early morning hours for maximum effectiveness.

Can I prune plants during hot weather?

Pruning is possible in hot weather but should be minimal—only remove dead branches and spent flowers. Use sharp pruning tools to make clean cuts that minimize plant stress. Avoid heavy pruning that removes excessive foliage needed to shade remaining plant tissue.

Why is soil preparation important for hot weather gardening?

Well-prepared soil with organic matter retains moisture better during heat. Hand cultivators and digging tools from Garden Guru Tools help you aerate and amend soil, improving water retention and nutrient availability when plants need maximum support.

What features should I look for in hot weather pruning tools?

Choose bypass pruners with sharp, durable blades that make clean cuts without crushing stems. Look for lightweight handles with ergonomic grips to reduce hand fatigue. Garden Guru Tools offers pruning equipment specifically designed for precision and comfort in hot conditions.
