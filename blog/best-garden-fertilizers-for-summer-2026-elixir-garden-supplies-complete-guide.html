Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start fertilizing my garden in summer?

Begin fertilizing in early summer (June for Northern Hemisphere) when plants are actively growing. Apply Elixir Garden Supplies' fertilizers every 2-4 weeks depending on plant type and product instructions for continuous nutrition.

What's the difference between Elixir Garden Supplies' organic and synthetic fertilizers?

Organic options release nutrients slowly as soil microbes break them down, while synthetic formulations provide immediate nutrient availability. Choose based on your gardening philosophy and timeline—both are effective with Elixir Garden Supplies' quality standards.

Can I use Elixir Garden Supplies fertilizers on all plants?

Elixir Garden Supplies offers specialized formulations for different plant types (vegetables, flowers, lawns, etc.). Always check product labels to ensure compatibility with your specific plants for optimal results.

How do I prevent over-fertilizing my garden?
