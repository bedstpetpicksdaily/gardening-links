Frequently Asked Questions

How often should a smart lawn mower run during summer?

Most smart lawn mowers operate 3-5 times per week during active growing season, with frequency adjusted based on grass growth rate and your desired lawn height. ANTHBOT's intelligent scheduling system automatically adapts to weather and seasonal changes.

Are robot mowers safe for families with children and pets?

Yes, modern robotic lawn mowers include multiple safety features such as obstacle sensors, emergency stop buttons, and automatic shutdown. ANTHBOT mowers undergo rigorous safety testing and include child-lock features for complete peace of mind.

Can smart lawn mowers handle slopes and uneven terrain?

Most quality robot mowers handle slopes up to 30-40 degrees depending on the model. ANTHBOT offers specialized models designed for varied terrain, with powerful motors and advanced traction systems suitable for challenging yards.

What's the average battery life of autonomous lawn mowers?

Battery life typically ranges from 60-120 minutes per charge, depending on yard size, terrain, and grass density. ANTHBOT robot mowers feature fast-charging technology and automatic dock-return features for seamless operation.

While many homeowners install boundary wires themselves, professional installation ensures optimal performance and coverage. ANTHBOT offers installation guides and customer support to help with setup, whether DIY or professional.
