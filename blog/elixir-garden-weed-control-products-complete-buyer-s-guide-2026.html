Are Elixir Garden weed control products safe for use around vegetables and edibles?

Elixir Garden offers both selective products safe for use near edibles and specialist formulations for other areas. Always check product labels carefully and maintain recommended buffer zones. Their organic range provides safer alternatives for vegetable gardens.

Can I use Elixir Garden products on newly seeded lawns?

Most herbicides should not be used on newly seeded lawns until grass is well-established (typically 4-6 weeks). Elixir Garden provides pre-emergent options for new lawns that prevent weeds before seeding, offering the best protection strategy.

What's the difference between selective and non-selective Elixir Garden weed killers?

Selective herbicides target specific weed types while protecting desired plants, ideal for lawns and borders. Non-selective products kill most vegetation they contact, perfect for clearing driveways, patios, and areas requiring complete vegetation removal.
