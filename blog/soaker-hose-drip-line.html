Frequently Asked Questions

How long do soaker hoses typically last?

Quality soaker hoses generally last 3-5 years with proper care. UV exposure and freeze-thaw cycles can reduce lifespan, so storing hoses indoors during winter extends durability significantly.

Can I use soaker hoses with a standard outdoor faucet?

Yes, soaker hoses connect easily to standard outdoor faucets. Most systems include necessary adapters and connectors, though you may want to add a timer for automated watering convenience.

What's the ideal water pressure for soaker hose systems?

Soaker hoses work best at 30 PSI (pounds per square inch). Standard household water pressure is usually adequate, but pressure regulators help optimize water distribution and prevent leaks.

Are soaker hoses suitable for all plant types?

Soaker hoses work excellently for vegetables, shrubs, trees, and flower beds. However, moisture-sensitive plants like succulents may require modified watering schedules to prevent overwatering.
