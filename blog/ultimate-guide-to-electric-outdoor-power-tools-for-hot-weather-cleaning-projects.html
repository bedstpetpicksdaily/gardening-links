Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use electric pressure washers during peak afternoon heat?

Yes, electric pressure washers from Giraffe Tools are specifically designed to operate safely in high temperatures. However, working in early morning or evening hours when temperatures are lower reduces personal discomfort and may improve water evaporation rates on surfaces you're cleaning.

How long do Giraffe Tools batteries last during hot weather?

Battery performance in hot weather typically decreases by 10-20% compared to moderate temperatures. Giraffe Tools batteries are engineered with thermal management to minimize heat-related degradation. Storing batteries in cool locations and avoiding direct sunlight extends runtime significantly.

Are electric tools from Giraffe Tools powerful enough for tough summer grime?

Absolutely. Giraffe Tools electric pressure washers deliver 1500-4000 PSI depending on the model, sufficient for removing algae, pollen, mud, and stubborn stains. Their adjustable pressure settings provide flexibility for different surface types while maintaining cleaning power.

What's the best maintenance schedule for electric outdoor tools during summer?

During heavy summer use, clean intake vents weekly to prevent dust accumulation, check batteries for proper charge, and inspect hoses for damage. Giraffe Tools equipment requires minimal seasonal maintenance—primarily storage in dry locations and battery management between uses.

Can I leave my Giraffe Tools equipment outside during hot weather?

Store equipment in shade or indoors when not in use. Prolonged sun exposure can degrade battery components and plastic housing. A simple garden shed or garage protects your investment while keeping tools accessible for frequent summer projects.
