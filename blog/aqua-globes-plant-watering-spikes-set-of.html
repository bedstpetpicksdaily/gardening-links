Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Aqua Globes keep plants watered?

Most users report 7-10 days of consistent moisture, depending on pot size, soil type, and plant species. Larger globes and clay pots extend watering intervals slightly. Test with a non-critical plant first to calibrate timing for your specific setup.

Will these work for outdoor garden beds?

They're designed for indoor containers and potted plants, not in-ground gardens. For raised beds or outdoor rows, drip irrigation systems or soaker hoses are more effective. These spikes excel in controlled indoor environments with contained soil.

Can you refill and reuse them multiple times?

Yes, they're reusable indefinitely. Fill by submerging the globe upside down in water until full, then insert into soil. The glass construction holds up to dozens of refills, though a few reviewers noted cracking with rough insertion—push gently at an angle.
