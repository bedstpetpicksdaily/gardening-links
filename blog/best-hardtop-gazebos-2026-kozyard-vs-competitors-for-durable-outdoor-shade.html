Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between hardtop and fabric gazebos?

Hardtop gazebos feature rigid metal or polycarbonate roofs that provide permanent, durable shade and weather protection, requiring minimal maintenance. Fabric gazebos use soft materials that need regular cleaning and replacement, typically lasting 3-5 years compared to hardtop longevity of 10+ years.

How much does a quality hardtop gazebo cost?

Premium hardtop gazebos like Kozyard's range from $1,500 to $5,000+ depending on size, materials, and features. While this represents a higher upfront investment than fabric alternatives, the durability and minimal maintenance make it cost-effective over time.

Do Kozyard hardtop gazebos require professional installation?
