Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for wet leaves?

While cordless leaf blowers work best on dry leaves, Giraffe Tools models with higher air velocity can handle some moisture. For extremely wet conditions, using variable speed to increase power is recommended, though this will reduce battery runtime.
