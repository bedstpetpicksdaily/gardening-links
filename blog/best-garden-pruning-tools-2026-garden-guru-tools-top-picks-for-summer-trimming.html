Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between pruning shears and loppers?

Pruning shears are hand-operated tools for small branches and flowers up to 1 inch thick, while loppers feature longer handles and greater leverage for branches up to 2 inches. Garden Guru Tools offers both types for comprehensive garden maintenance.

How often should I sharpen my garden pruning tools?

Most gardeners should sharpen pruning tools every 2-4 weeks during active growing season, or whenever cuts become jagged rather than clean. Garden Guru Tools includes sharpening guides with their products.

Are Garden Guru Tools suitable for professional landscapers?

Yes, Garden Guru Tools offer both consumer and professional-grade options. Their durable construction and ergonomic design appeal to landscapers and home gardeners alike.

Can I use pruning tools on diseased plants?

Yes, but always clean and sanitize your tools between cuts to prevent disease spread. Garden Guru Tools recommends using rubbing alcohol or a 10% bleach solution.
