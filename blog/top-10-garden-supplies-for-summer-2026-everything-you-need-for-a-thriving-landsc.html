Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start applying summer fertilizers?

Begin fertilizing in late spring (May) as plants enter their active growing phase. Continue applications throughout summer according to product instructions. Elixir Garden Supplies provides clear guidance on application schedules for optimal results.

Is chemical weed control safe for vegetable gardens?

Elixir offers both chemical and organic weed control solutions. For vegetable gardens, many gardeners prefer organic options. Always read product labels carefully and follow application instructions to ensure safety around edible plants.

How often should I apply plant food during summer?

Application frequency depends on your specific plant types and fertilizer formulation. Slow-release products from Elixir Garden Supplies may only need application once or twice per season, while liquid fertilizers might be needed weekly. Check product instructions for your plants' specific needs.

Can I mix different garden chemicals together?

Never mix garden chemicals unless specifically directed on product labels. Mixing can create dangerous reactions or reduce effectiveness. Elixir Garden Supplies provides clear instructions—always follow them precisely.

What's the best time to apply weed control?

Pre-emergent weed control works best in early spring, while post-emergent solutions are most effective on actively growing weeds. Summer heat can affect product performance, so apply early morning or late evening for best results.
