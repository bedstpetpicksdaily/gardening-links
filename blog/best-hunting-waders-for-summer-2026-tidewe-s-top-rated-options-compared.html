Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between breathable and neoprene waders for summer?

Breathable waders use specialized fabrics that allow moisture vapor to escape while maintaining waterproofing, keeping you dry and cool in warm conditions. Neoprene waders are insulated and designed for cold water, making them heavy and uncomfortable for summer hunting. TideWe's breathable options are specifically engineered for warm-weather performance.

How do I choose between chest waders, hip waders, and bootfoot waders?

Chest waders offer maximum coverage for deep water hunting, hip waders work well for shallow water and reduced weight, and bootfoot waders eliminate the need for separate hunting boots. Your choice depends on water depth, terrain type, and personal preference. TideWe offers all three options to suit different hunting scenarios.

How long do TideWe summer waders typically last?
