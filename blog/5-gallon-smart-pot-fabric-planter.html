Frequently Asked Questions

What size plants can I grow in a 5-gallon fabric pot?

5-gallon pots work best for medium vegetables (tomatoes, peppers, squash), herbs, and small fruit plants. They're ideal for single-stem plants and provide enough soil volume for strong root development.

How often should I water fabric pots?

Fabric pots require more frequent watering than plastic containers due to increased evaporation. Water when the top inch of soil feels dry, typically every 1-2 days during warm weather.

Can I reuse 5-gallon fabric planters?

Yes, fabric pots are highly reusable. Clean them between seasons and they'll last 3-5 years or longer with proper storage and care away from direct sunlight.

What soil should I use with fabric planters?

Use high-quality potting mix, not garden soil. Quality mixes ensure proper drainage, aeration, and nutrient availability that fabric pots are designed to maximize.
