Frequently Asked Questions

What size retractable hose reel do I need for my garden?

Choose based on your garden's layout and farthest watering point. Hoselink offers 30-meter, 40-meter, and 50-meter models. Measure from your water source to the farthest corner of your garden and add 10-15 meters for maneuverability. Larger properties benefit from 50-meter reels, while small to medium gardens work well with 30-40 meter options.

Are retractable hose reels worth the investment?

Yes. Quality retractable reels like Hoselink's protect your hose from UV damage, eliminate tangling, save storage space, and last 7-10 years with proper care. The convenience factor alone—automatic retraction and organized storage—makes them worthwhile for frequent gardeners. Premium reels also integrate with watering accessories, sprinklers, and garden tools for seamless system integration.

Can I use Hoselink reels with standard garden hoses and sprinklers?

Hoselink reels are compatible with most standard garden hose fittings and professional watering accessories. However, Hoselink offers optimized sprinklers, watering wands, and garden tools specifically designed to work seamlessly with their reel systems, providing superior performance and durability when used together as an integrated solution.

How do I maintain my retractable hose reel during summer?

Store reels in shade when not in use, rinse hoses after each watering to remove dirt, check retraction mechanisms monthly, and ensure drainage holes are clear. Hoselink reels feature weather-resistant materials that handle summer heat, but proper storage and occasional cleaning extend lifespan significantly and maintain smooth operation.
