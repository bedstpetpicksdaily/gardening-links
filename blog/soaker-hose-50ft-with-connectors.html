Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a 50ft soaker hose last?

Quality soaker hoses typically last 3-5 years with proper care, though lifespan depends on UV exposure, water pressure, and climate conditions.

Can I connect multiple soaker hoses together?

Yes, most soaker hoses come with connectors that allow you to link multiple hoses together to extend coverage across larger garden areas.

What's the difference between soaker hoses and drip irrigation?

Soaker hoses release water along their entire length, while drip irrigation uses emitters at specific points. Soaker hoses are simpler and more affordable for most home gardens.

Is a soaker hose better than overhead sprinklers?

Yes, soaker hoses use 30-50% less water than sprinklers, deliver water directly to roots, and reduce leaf diseases caused by wet foliage.
