Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start using summer fertilizers?

Begin fertilizing once temperatures consistently exceed 70°F (21°C) and active growth resumes. Elixir Garden Supplies recommends starting applications in late spring to build plant strength before peak summer heat arrives.

How often should I apply Elixir Garden fertilizers?

Liquid formulas typically require weekly to bi-weekly application, while Elixir Garden's slow-release granules provide nutrition for 6-8 weeks. Always follow product-specific instructions for best results.

Can I use the same fertilizer for all my plants?

While Elixir Garden offers versatile all-purpose formulas, specialized products exist for specific needs. Vegetables, flowers, and ornamentals have different nutrient requirements, so choosing targeted solutions yields better results.
