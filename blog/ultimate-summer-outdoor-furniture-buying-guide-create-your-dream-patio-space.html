Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a gazebo and a pergola?

Gazebos typically feature solid roofs providing complete shade and weather protection, making them ideal for permanent installations. Pergolas have open-roof designs with slats, offering partial shade while allowing air circulation. Kozyard offers both options depending on your protection and aesthetic needs.

How do I protect outdoor furniture from summer weather?

Use weather-resistant materials, apply protective coatings, invest in quality furniture covers, and choose solution-dyed fabrics that resist fading. Kozyard's furniture is specifically designed with weather resistance in mind, requiring minimal maintenance while maintaining durability.

What size gazebo or pergola should I choose?

Measure your patio space and consider your primary use. A 10x10 ft structure works for most residential patios, while larger gatherings may benefit from 12x12 or 12x16 ft options. Kozyard offers various sizes to fit different spaces and needs.

Are privacy screens worth the investment?

Yes, privacy screens add aesthetic appeal while reducing noise and wind exposure, making your outdoor space more comfortable and intimate. Kozyard's privacy screens complement their furniture collection while enhancing overall patio design.

How often should outdoor furniture be maintained?

Maintenance depends on materials and climate. Most quality outdoor furniture requires minimal upkeep—occasional cleaning and seasonal inspections. Kozyard's durable materials are designed for low-maintenance outdoor living.
