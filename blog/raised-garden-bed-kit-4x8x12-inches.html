Frequently Asked Questions

What size soil bag do I need for a 4x8x12 raised garden bed?

A 4x8x12 inch bed holds approximately 32 cubic feet of soil. You'll need about 32 bags of standard potting mix or garden soil, depending on the brand's coverage.

Can I use a 4x8x12 raised bed kit year-round?

Yes, raised beds can be used year-round. In winter, consider adding cold frames or row covers to protect plants from frost and extend your growing season.

Does the metal raised bed kit come with drainage holes?

Most metal kits have a solid bottom, so water naturally drains through the soil and out the sides. Add landscape fabric if moisture retention is a concern.

How long does a metal raised garden bed kit last?

Quality metal raised beds typically last 10-15+ years. Galvanized or powder-coated finishes provide better rust resistance and longevity than bare metal.
