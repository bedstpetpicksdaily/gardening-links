FAQ: Common Compost Bin Questions

What can you put in a compost bin?

Greens include fruit scraps, vegetable peels, coffee grounds, and grass clippings. Browns include dry leaves, shredded paper, cardboard, and wood chips. Avoid meat, dairy, and oils. A good mix is roughly 3 parts brown to 1 part green.

How long does compost take to be ready?

Traditional stationary bins take 6-12 months, tumblers speed this up to 4-8 weeks with regular turning, and electric composters finish in 3-8 hours. The actual timeline depends on temperature, moisture, and material size.
