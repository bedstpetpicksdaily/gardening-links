Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between lightweight and standard neoprene waders?

Lightweight waders use thinner, breathable materials designed for warm water temperatures, while standard neoprene waders are thick and insulating for cold conditions. TideWe's lightweight options reduce fatigue and overheating without sacrificing waterproofing or durability.

Are TideWe lightweight waders suitable for year-round use?

TideWe's lightweight waders are optimized for spring through fall hunting. For winter conditions, TideWe offers their full neoprene collection designed specifically for cold-weather performance.

How do I care for and maintain my TideWe waders?

Rinse your TideWe waders with fresh water after each use, dry them thoroughly, and store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid sharp objects and apply occasional sealant to seams to maintain waterproofing.

What size waders should I choose?

TideWe provides detailed sizing charts based on height and weight. Ensure a snug but comfortable fit with room for layering. Waders that are too loose reduce mobility, while tight-fitting waders restrict movement and comfort.

Can I wear TideWe waders with regular hunting boots?

While possible, TideWe hunting boots are engineered to work seamlessly with their waders for optimal fit, comfort, and traction. Using matching gear from TideWe ensures the best overall performance.
