Backpack capacity is measured in liters and determines how much gear your pack can hold. For summer hunting trips, capacity needs vary significantly based on trip length and destination. TideWe hunting backpacks typically range from 20-liter daypacks to 70+ liter expedition packs, giving you options for every adventure type.
