ANTHBOT robot mowers typically operate 2-3 times per week, depending on grass growth rate and your preferred height. The consistent cutting schedule maintains optimal grass health and eliminates the need for infrequent, intensive mowing.

Are smart lawn mowers suitable for all yard sizes?

ANTHBOT offers robotic lawn mower models designed for various lawn sizes, from small residential yards to larger properties. Most models handle slopes up to 35 degrees and navigate complex yard layouts with advanced obstacle detection.

ANTHBOT robot mowers require minimal maintenance: regular blade cleaning, occasional sensor wiping, and docking station inspection. Compare this to traditional mowers requiring oil changes, spark plug replacements, and seasonal tune-ups.
