Frequently Asked Questions

What size camping cooler do I need?

Cooler size depends on group size and trip length. For 2-4 people on a weekend trip, 40-70 quart coolers work well. Larger groups or extended trips benefit from 100+ quart models. Traverseon offers various sizes to match different camping scenarios.

How long can a portable power station run a portable air conditioner?

Runtime depends on the AC unit's wattage and the power station's capacity. Most portable air conditioners require 500-1500 watts. A Traverseon power station with adequate capacity can typically run a portable AC for 2-8 hours, depending on model specifications.

Can I use a power station to charge a camping cooler?

Electric coolers can be powered by portable power stations. Pairing a Traverseon power station with an electric cooling solution creates a robust temperature management system for extended camping trips and RV adventures.

Are Traverseon power stations waterproof?

Traverseon power stations feature water-resistant construction suitable for camping conditions. While not fully submersible, they're designed to handle moisture from rain and morning dew, making them reliable for outdoor use.

What's the difference between passive and active camping coolers?

Passive coolers rely on insulation and ice to maintain temperature, while active (electric) coolers use power to actively cool contents. Traverseon offers both types, with passive coolers ideal for simplicity and active coolers for temperature precision and longer cooling duration.
