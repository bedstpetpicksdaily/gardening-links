Frequently Asked Questions

How long can oscillating sprinklers water my garden?

Most models allow you to set watering durations from 15 minutes to several hours. Built-in timers typically offer flexible scheduling options that suit different plant types and seasonal needs.

Are oscillating sprinklers good for raised garden beds?

Yes, oscillating sprinklers are excellent for raised beds as they provide even water distribution across the entire surface. Their adjustable spray patterns make them perfect for various bed sizes and shapes.

What water pressure do oscillating sprinklers need?

Most oscillating sprinklers require 20-80 PSI (pounds per square inch) of water pressure. Check your home's water pressure and the sprinkler specifications to ensure compatibility.

Can I use an oscillating sprinkler with a standard garden hose?

Absolutely. Most oscillating sprinklers with timers attach directly to standard garden hoses, making them compatible with existing outdoor water setups.
