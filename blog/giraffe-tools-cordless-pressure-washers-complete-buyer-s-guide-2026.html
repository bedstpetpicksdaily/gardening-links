Frequently Asked Questions

What PSI do I need for residential pressure washing?

For most residential applications, 2000-3000 PSI is ideal. Giraffe Tools cordless models typically deliver 320-600 PSI, which is suitable for light to medium-duty cleaning tasks like patios, garden furniture, and vehicles. For heavy-duty concrete cleaning, higher PSI models are recommended.

How long do Giraffe Tools cordless pressure washer batteries last?

Battery runtime typically ranges from 20-40 minutes depending on the model and pressure setting used. Higher pressure settings consume battery faster. Most Giraffe Tools batteries charge fully within 30-60 minutes, and purchasing additional batteries allows for extended cleaning sessions.

Are cordless pressure washers suitable for all outdoor cleaning tasks?

Cordless pressure washers like those from Giraffe Tools excel at light to medium-duty cleaning. They're perfect for patios, decks, fences, vehicles, and garden equipment. For extreme heavy-duty commercial applications, gas-powered models may be more suitable, but Giraffe Tools cordless options handle most residential needs effectively.

How do I maintain my Giraffe Tools cordless pressure washer?

Maintenance is minimal compared to gas models. Simply flush the system after use, store the battery in a cool place, and clean nozzles regularly. Check the product manual for specific care instructions, and ensure your battery receives periodic maintenance charging during off-season storage.

What attachments and accessories are compatible with Giraffe Tools pressure washers?

Giraffe Tools cordless pressure washers support multiple nozzles for different spray patterns, foam cannon attachments, extension wands, and various hose connections. Check your specific model's specifications to ensure compatibility with third-party accessories.
