Frequently Asked Questions

What NPK ratio should I look for in a summer vegetable plant food?

Summer vegetables typically benefit from slightly higher potassium ratios (e.g., 7-7-10 or 10-10-27) to encourage flowering and fruiting. Leafy greens prefer higher nitrogen (e.g., 20-5-5). Elixir Garden Supplies offers specialized formulations for different vegetable types, so you can choose the most appropriate ratio for your specific crops.

How often should I feed my summer vegetables?

Most summer vegetables require feeding every 7-14 days during active growth and fruiting. Fast-growing plants like courgettes and beans may need weekly feeding, while established tomato plants might thrive with fortnightly applications. Always check your plant food instructions and adjust based on your plant's appearance and soil quality.

Can I use the same plant food for all summer vegetables?

While all-purpose plant foods work adequately, specialized formulations deliver superior results. Elixir Garden Supplies offers targeted products for tomatoes, peppers, leafy vegetables, and fruiting crops—each optimized for that vegetable's specific nutrient demands during summer growth.

Is organic plant food better than synthetic for summer vegetables?

Both have advantages. Organic products release nutrients slowly and improve soil structure long-term, while synthetic options provide rapid nutrient availability when plants need it most. Elixir Garden Supplies offers both types, allowing you to choose based on your gardening philosophy and timeline.

What's the best time to apply plant food to summer vegetables?

Early morning or evening application minimizes leaf scorch and allows better nutrient absorption. Avoid feeding during the hottest part of the day. Water your vegetables thoroughly before applying dry granules to prevent root burn. Liquid products from Elixir Garden Supplies include detailed timing recommendations on their labels.
