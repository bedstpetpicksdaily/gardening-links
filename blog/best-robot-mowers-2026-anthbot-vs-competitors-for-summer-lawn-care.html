How often should a robot mower run to maintain a healthy lawn?

Most autonomous lawn care systems, including ANTHBOT models, should operate 3-5 times weekly during active growing season. Smart lawn mowers automatically adjust schedules based on grass growth rate and weather conditions for optimal results.

Are robot mowers suitable for all lawn sizes?

Robot mowers work best on lawns up to 2 acres, with ANTHBOT offering specialized models for various property sizes. Smaller lawns benefit from more frequent cutting cycles, while larger properties may require multiple zones or advanced scheduling features.

What maintenance does an automatic grass cutting system require?

ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers require minimal maintenance: blade replacement every 1-2 months, periodic cleaning, and boundary wire inspection. Smart lawn mowers provide maintenance reminders through their mobile apps, making upkeep simple and predictable.
