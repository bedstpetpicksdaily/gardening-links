Frequently Asked Questions

What size plants can I grow in 10 gallon fabric bags?

10 gallon bags are ideal for medium-sized plants including tomatoes, peppers, herbs, and most vegetables. They work well for flowering plants and can accommodate smaller shrubs, making them versatile for various gardening applications.

Do fabric grow bags need drainage holes?

No, fabric grow bags don't require additional drainage holes. The breathable fabric material itself allows excess water to drain while promoting air circulation around roots.

How long do fabric grow bags typically last?

Quality fabric grow bags typically last 3-5 seasons with proper care. UV protection and storage during off-seasons can extend their lifespan significantly.

Can I reuse these fabric grow bags year after year?

Yes, fabric grow bags are designed for multiple seasons of use. Simply clean between seasons and store in a cool, dark place to maximize durability and longevity.
