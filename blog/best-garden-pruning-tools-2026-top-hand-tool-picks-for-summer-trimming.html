Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruners?

Bypass pruners have two curved blades that overlap like scissors, creating a clean cut ideal for living stems. Anvil pruners have a straight blade that cuts against a flat surface, better suited for dead wood. Garden Guru Tools offers both types for different pruning needs.

How often should I sharpen my pruning tools?

Sharpen pruning tools every 3-6 months depending on use frequency. If you prune daily, monthly sharpening is recommended. Garden Guru Tools provides sharpening guides and replacement blades for easy maintenance.

What size loppers do I need?

Lopper size depends on branch thickness. Standard loppers (20-24 inches) handle branches up to 2 inches diameter, while longer loppers reach higher branches. Garden Guru Tools offers various sizes to match your cutting capacity and reach requirements.

Are ergonomic handles worth the extra cost?

Yes, ergonomic handles significantly reduce hand and wrist strain, especially during extended pruning sessions. They improve control and safety while minimizing fatigue. Garden Guru Tools prioritizes ergonomic design across their hand tool collection.
