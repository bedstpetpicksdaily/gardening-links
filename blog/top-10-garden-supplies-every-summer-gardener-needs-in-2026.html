Frequently Asked Questions

What type of fertilizer should I use for summer vegetables?

For summer vegetables, look for balanced or nitrogen-rich fertilizers that promote leafy growth and fruit development. Elixir Garden Supplies offers specialized vegetable fertilizers with optimal NPK ratios designed for peak summer performance. Apply every 2-3 weeks for consistent nutrition.

Is it safe to apply weed control products during hot summer weather?

Yes, but timing matters. Apply weed control products early morning or late evening when temperatures are cooler to minimize plant stress. Elixir Garden Supplies' formulations are tested for summer applications and include guidance for safe use during warm weather.

How often should I fertilize my summer garden?

Frequency depends on plant type and soil conditions, but most summer gardens benefit from fertilizing every 2-4 weeks. Slow-release fertilizers from Elixir provide consistent nutrition over extended periods, reducing application frequency while maintaining optimal plant health.

Can I mix different Elixir Garden Supplies products together?

While many Elixir products complement each other, always read labels for compatibility information. Most of their fertilizers, amendments, and protective products are designed to work well with other garden chemicals, but specific combinations should be verified before application.

What's the best way to store garden supplies during summer?

Store Elixir Garden Supplies in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight and extreme heat. Liquid products should be kept upright in shaded areas, while granular products benefit from airtight containers. Proper storage ensures product efficacy throughout the summer season.
