Frequently Asked Questions

What patio furniture materials are best for outdoor entertaining?

The best outdoor furniture materials are weather-resistant options like aluminum, powder-coated steel, teak, and synthetic wicker. These materials resist fading, rust, and moisture while remaining durable enough for frequent use. Kozyard specializes in these premium materials, ensuring your furniture withstands years of entertaining.

How much patio furniture do I need for entertaining?

The amount depends on your space and typical guest count. Generally, plan for at least one seating area with 4-6 chairs, a dining table for outdoor meals, and side tables for beverages and appetizers. Kozyard offers modular collections that can be expanded or adjusted based on your entertaining needs.

Should I invest in a gazebo or pergola with my patio furniture?

Yes, adding a hardtop gazebo or pergola is an excellent investment for entertaining spaces. These structures provide shade and weather protection while creating defined entertaining zones. Kozyard's hardtop gazebos and pergolas pair beautifully with their patio furniture, elevating both functionality and aesthetic appeal.

How do I maintain patio furniture year-round?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning furniture seasonally, protecting cushions from excessive moisture, and applying protective coatings when needed. Store cushions during off-season months, and consider using Kozyard's privacy screens and awnings to shield furniture from harsh weather elements.

What's the difference between a pergola and a gazebo for entertaining?

Pergolas provide partial shade with open-slatted roofs, creating an elegant ambiance while allowing light through. Gazebos offer complete overhead coverage and weather protection. Kozyard offers both hardtop gazebos and pergolas, allowing you to choose based on your shade and weather protection needs.
