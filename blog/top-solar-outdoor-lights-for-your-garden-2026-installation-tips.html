Installation Tips for Maximum Solar Light Performance

Proper installation is crucial for solar light effectiveness. Position lights in areas receiving direct sunlight for at least 6-8 hours daily to ensure full battery charging. Avoid shaded spots under trees or near structures that block morning and afternoon sun. Clean solar panels monthly to prevent dirt and dust buildup that reduces charging efficiency. When planning your garden lighting layout, consider pathways where you've installed Hoselink sprinkler systems—positioning lights to illuminate these areas improves safety and highlights your garden's water features. Space lights appropriately based on their brightness rating: typically 1.5-2 meters apart for pathway lighting and 2-3 meters for ambient illumination.

How long do solar garden lights typically last?

Can solar lights work in cloudy climates?

Yes, though performance improves in sunnier conditions. Even cloudy days provide sufficient ambient light for charging. Position lights in the sunniest spots available for optimal battery performance.

How do I maintain solar outdoor lights?

Clean solar panels monthly, check battery connections yearly, and replace batteries every 2-3 years as needed. Hoselink provides maintenance guides to help extend light lifespan and maximize performance.

What's the best placement strategy for garden solar lights?
