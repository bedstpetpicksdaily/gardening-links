Frequently Asked Questions

How long do soaker hoses last?

Most quality soaker hoses last 3-5 years with proper care. UV exposure and temperature fluctuations can shorten lifespan, so store hoses indoors during winter and use hose covers in direct sunlight.

Can I use soaker hoses with raised garden beds?

Yes, soaker hoses are ideal for raised beds. You can weave them through multiple rows or arrange them in loops to ensure even water distribution across the entire bed.

Do I need a timer for soaker hose irrigation?

While not required, a timer is highly recommended. It allows you to set consistent watering schedules, which is especially helpful during hot weather or when you're away from home.

What water pressure do soaker hoses need?

Most soaker hoses work best with 25-60 PSI water pressure. Check your hose specifications, as some are designed for low-pressure systems while others require standard household pressure.

How often should I water with a soaker hose?

Watering frequency depends on your climate, soil type, and plants. Most gardens benefit from 1-2 deep watering sessions per week, delivering about 1-2 inches of water per week.
