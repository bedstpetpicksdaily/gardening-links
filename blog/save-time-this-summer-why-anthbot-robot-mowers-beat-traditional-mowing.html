Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an ANTHBOT robot mower run on a single charge?

Most ANTHBOT models can mow for 2-3 hours before automatically returning to their charging dock. This is typically enough to maintain lawns up to 1 acre, depending on the model and grass conditions.

Can ANTHBOT robot mowers handle wet grass and rain?

Yes, ANTHBOT's robotic lawn mowers are weatherproof and can operate in light rain and damp grass. However, they'll automatically pause during heavy rain to protect the equipment and prevent turf damage.

Do I need to install a boundary wire for ANTHBOT robot mowers?

Most ANTHBOT models use boundary wires or markers to define the mowing area. Installation is straightforward and typically takes a few hours, but some newer models offer GPS-based boundary options.

How much maintenance do ANTHBOT robot mowers require?

ANTHBOT robot mowers require minimal maintenance—mainly cleaning the deck and checking blades regularly. There's no oil changes, spark plugs, or seasonal storage like traditional mowers.

Are ANTHBOT robot mowers safe around pets and children?

Yes, ANTHBOT mowers have safety sensors and can be scheduled to operate when children and pets aren't in the yard. They're also much quieter than traditional mowers, reducing stress for animals.
