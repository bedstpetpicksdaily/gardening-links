Frequently Asked Questions

How does the self-watering system in the Bloem Ariana work?

The planter features a water reservoir at the base that wicks moisture up to plant roots through a special system, maintaining consistent soil moisture without daily watering. Simply fill the reservoir, and it gradually waters your plants over time.

What size plants can fit in the 12-inch Ariana planter?

The 12-inch size is perfect for medium-sized plants, including vegetables, herbs, small shrubs, and perennials. It provides ample soil depth and volume for healthy root development.

Can I use the Bloem Ariana for outdoor and indoor plants?

Yes, the Ariana's UV-resistant plastic makes it suitable for both outdoor gardens and indoor spaces. It's versatile enough for patios, balconies, decks, and sunny windowsills.

How often do I need to refill the water reservoir?

Depending on climate, sunlight, and plant type, the reservoir typically lasts 1-2 weeks. Hot, sunny conditions may require more frequent refilling than cooler seasons.
