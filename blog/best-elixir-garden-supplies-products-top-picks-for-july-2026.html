Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to apply Elixir Garden Supplies fertilizers?

Most Elixir fertilizers work best when applied in early morning or late evening to prevent leaf burn and maximize nutrient absorption. For best results, follow the application instructions on each product, which typically recommend application every 2-4 weeks during the growing season.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies products safe for organic gardens?

Elixir offers both conventional and organic-certified product lines. Check the packaging or product description to identify organic options if you're maintaining an organic certification. Many of their plant foods and some pest control solutions are suitable for organic gardening practices.

How quickly will I see results from Elixir weed control products?

Most Elixir weed control solutions show visible results within 3-7 days of application. Contact herbicides typically work fastest, while systemic herbicides may take slightly longer as they work through the plant's system. Always allow adequate drying time after application for optimal effectiveness.

Can I use multiple Elixir Garden Supplies products together?

Yes, Elixir products are designed to work well together as part of a complete garden care program. However, always read product labels carefully and follow mixing instructions. Never combine different chemical treatments without consulting the guidelines or contacting customer service.

What's the shelf life of Elixir Garden Supplies products?

Most Elixir products have a shelf life of 2-3 years when stored in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight. Liquid products typically last 18-24 months, while granular fertilizers tend to last longer. Always check the expiration date before use for maximum effectiveness.
