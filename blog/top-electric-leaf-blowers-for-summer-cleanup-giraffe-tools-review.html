Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Giraffe Tools leaf blower battery last?

Battery runtime varies by model and usage intensity, typically lasting 20-60 minutes on a single charge. Variable speed settings help extend runtime for lighter cleanup tasks.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for large properties?

Yes, Giraffe Tools offers multiple models with varying power levels. For larger properties, consider purchasing additional batteries or choosing models with extended runtime capabilities.

Can I use Giraffe Tools batteries with other outdoor equipment?

Many Giraffe Tools products feature interchangeable battery systems, allowing you to use the same batteries across their range of electric outdoor power tools including pressure washers and snow blowers.

Is maintenance required for electric leaf blowers?

Electric leaf blowers require minimal maintenance compared to gas models. Simply keep air intake vents clean and store the battery properly during off-seasons.

What is the noise level of Giraffe Tools leaf blowers?

Electric leaf blowers operate at significantly lower noise levels (typically 60-75 dB) compared to gas models, making them suitable for residential neighborhoods.
