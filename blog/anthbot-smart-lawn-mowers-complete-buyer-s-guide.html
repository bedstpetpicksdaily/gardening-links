Yes, ANTHBOT autonomous lawn care models handle slopes up to specific angles (typically 35-40% depending on model). Always check your specific model's specifications and terrain compatibility before installation.

What maintenance do ANTHBOT smart lawn mowers require?

Regular maintenance includes monthly blade checks, quarterly blade replacement, and annual professional servicing. ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers require significantly less maintenance than traditional mowers, primarily involving blade sharpening and minor cleaning.
