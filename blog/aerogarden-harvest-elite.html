Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to replace the nutrient pods?

AeroGarden nutrient pods typically last for one complete grow cycle, usually 6-12 months depending on plant type and usage.

Can I use seeds instead of AeroGarden pods?

Yes, you can purchase universal seed pods separately and start seeds from scratch, though official AeroGarden seed pods have the highest success rates.

What plants can I grow in the Harvest Elite?

You can grow herbs, lettuce, peppers, tomatoes, flowers, and other compatible plants using approved AeroGarden seed pods or compatible alternatives.

Is the AeroGarden Harvest Elite noisy?

The system operates quietly with minimal noise from the water pump, making it suitable for bedrooms, kitchens, and office spaces.
