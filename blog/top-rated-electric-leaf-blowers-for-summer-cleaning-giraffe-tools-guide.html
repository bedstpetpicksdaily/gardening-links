Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Giraffe Tools electric leaf blower battery last?

Battery runtime varies by model and usage intensity, typically ranging from 20 to 60 minutes per charge. Most Giraffe Tools models support fast-charging batteries that recharge in 30-60 minutes, ideal for standard residential yard work.

Are electric leaf blowers as powerful as gas models?

Yes, modern electric leaf blowers like those from Giraffe Tools deliver comparable air speeds and power to gas models. Advanced brushless motor technology ensures consistent, dependable performance without the environmental impact.

Can I use a Giraffe Tools leaf blower on wet leaves?

While electric leaf blowers work best on dry leaves, many Giraffe Tools models can handle lightly damp debris. For heavily wet conditions, allow leaves to dry first for optimal performance and motor protection.

What maintenance do electric leaf blowers require?

Electric leaf blowers require minimal maintenance—simply keep air vents clean, store batteries properly, and inspect for damage seasonally. No oil changes, spark plugs, or fuel system maintenance needed.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for commercial use?

Giraffe Tools offers both residential and commercial-grade equipment. While some models are perfect for homeowners, their professional-grade leaf blowers are built for contractor and commercial landscaping applications.
