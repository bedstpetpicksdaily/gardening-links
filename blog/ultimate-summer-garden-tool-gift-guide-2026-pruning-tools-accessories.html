Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruners?

Bypass pruners work like scissors with two curved blades that pass each other, ideal for green stems and living branches. Anvil pruners have a straight blade that closes onto a flat surface, better suited for dead wood. Garden Guru Tools offers both types for different gardening needs.

How often should I sharpen my pruning tools?

For regular use, sharpen your pruning tools every 1-2 months. Garden Guru Tools provides sharpening guides with their products, and dull blades can damage plants and increase injury risk, so maintaining sharpness is essential.
