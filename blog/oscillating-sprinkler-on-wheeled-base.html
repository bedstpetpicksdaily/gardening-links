Frequently Asked Questions

How wide an area can an oscillating sprinkler cover?

Most oscillating sprinklers cover between 30-50 feet in length and 15-30 feet in width, depending on water pressure and nozzle settings. Check product specifications for exact coverage dimensions.

Can I use an oscillating sprinkler on a sloped garden?

Yes, but wheeled base sprinklers work best on level ground. For slopes, position the sprinkler at the lowest point and adjust spray patterns to ensure even water distribution.

How often should I water my garden with an oscillating sprinkler?

Most gardens benefit from 1-2 inches of water per week. Run your sprinkler for 30-60 minutes depending on coverage area and soil type, adjusting based on rainfall and plant needs.

Are wheeled oscillating sprinklers suitable for raised garden beds?

Wheeled sprinklers work best for larger lawn areas. For raised beds, consider stationary oscillating or drip irrigation systems for more targeted watering.
