Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruners?

Bypass pruners cut with two sharp blades like scissors, ideal for green, living stems. Anvil pruners have one sharp blade that cuts against a flat surface, better for deadwood and thicker branches. Garden Guru Tools offers both styles to match your specific pruning needs.

How often should I maintain my pruning tools?

Clean your pruning tools after each use to prevent disease transmission. Sharpen blades every few months of regular use, and oil moving parts seasonally. Garden Guru Tools manufactures equipment that's easy to maintain, extending tool life significantly.
