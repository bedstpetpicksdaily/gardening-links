Application Tips for Maximum Heat-Weather Effectiveness

Timing your application is crucial when using any weed control product in hot weather. Early morning or late evening applications—when temperatures are lower—allow better absorption before peak heat arrives. Elixir Garden Supplies recommends checking soil moisture before application; slightly damp soil enhances herbicide uptake compared to bone-dry conditions.

For best results with Elixir products, avoid applying during extreme heat waves (above 30°C) as this can stress the application process, though the formulas themselves remain stable. Always read the specific instructions on your chosen Elixir Garden product, as different formulations may have slightly different optimal conditions.
