Frequently Asked Questions

Are TideWe breathable waders truly waterproof?

Yes. TideWe's breathable waders utilize waterproof membranes bonded to breathable outer shells. The technology allows moisture vapor to escape while blocking liquid water, keeping you dry from both perspiration and external water exposure.

What temperature range are TideWe hot-weather waders suitable for?

TideWe's summer wader collection performs optimally in temperatures above 60°F. The lightweight construction and ventilation features make them ideal for hunts where temperatures exceed 70°F, though they remain functional in cooler conditions.

How do TideWe stockingfoot waders compare to bootfoot models for summer hunting?

Stockingfoot waders offer superior mobility and packability, making them ideal for extreme heat or backcountry hunts. Bootfoot waders provide integrated protection and convenience. TideWe manufactures both styles—choose based on your specific hunting environment and preferences.

Can I pair TideWe waders with any hunting boots?

TideWe stockingfoot waders work with any wading boots featuring compatible sizing. TideWe also manufactures premium hunting boots specifically designed to complement their wader collection, ensuring optimal fit and performance together.

How should I maintain TideWe breathable waders?
