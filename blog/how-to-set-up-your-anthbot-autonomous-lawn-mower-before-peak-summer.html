Step 2: Install the Boundary Wire for Optimal Lawn Coverage

The boundary wire is crucial for your ANTHBOT robot mower's navigation system. Lay out the perimeter wire around your lawn's edges, ensuring it follows the natural boundaries of your yard. ANTHBOT's autonomous lawn care technology uses this wire to create a virtual fence, preventing your smart lawn mower from wandering into unwanted areas like gardens, driveways, or pools.

Secure the wire with the provided stakes every 1-2 feet, keeping it flush with the ground to prevent tripping hazards. For complex lawn layouts with multiple zones, carefully plan wire placement to optimize coverage. ANTHBOT's robotic lawn mowers efficiently navigate even irregularly shaped yards when the boundary wire is properly installed, ensuring comprehensive automatic grass cutting across your entire lawn.
