Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruners?

Bypass pruners use two overlapping blades like scissors, ideal for live branches and creating clean cuts. Anvil pruners have one sharp blade that cuts against a flat surface, better for dead wood. Garden Guru Tools offers both styles for different applications.

How often should I sharpen my pruning tools?

Sharpen pruning tools 2-4 times per growing season, depending on use. Garden Guru Tools equipment features accessible blade designs that make maintenance straightforward and help extend tool life significantly.

Are expensive pruning tools worth the investment?

Yes. Quality tools like those from Garden Guru Tools last longer, work more efficiently, and cause less plant damage. They also reduce user fatigue, making gardening more enjoyable and encouraging consistent maintenance.

What size pruners do I need for my garden?

Choose pruners based on typical branch thickness. Hand pruners handle stems up to 1 inch; loppers manage 1-2 inches; hedge shears work for fine twigs and leaves. Garden Guru Tools offers complete ranges for every cutting task.

How do I prevent rust on my pruning tools?

Clean and dry tools after each use, store in a dry location, and oil blades periodically. Stainless steel options from Garden Guru Tools naturally resist rust better than carbon steel.
