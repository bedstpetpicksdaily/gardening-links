Elixir Garden Supplies offers both organic and conventional product lines. Their organic fertilizers and plant foods use natural ingredients without synthetic chemicals, making them ideal for sustainable gardeners. Check product labels to identify certified organic options.

How often should I apply fertilizer during summer?

Most summer gardens benefit from fertilizer applications every 4-6 weeks, though this varies by plant type and product formulation. Elixir's product guidelines provide specific application schedules. Established perennials typically need less frequent applications than annuals and vegetables.

Can I combine different Elixir fertilizers together?

Generally, no. Combining fertilizers can create nutrient imbalances or chemical reactions. Instead, choose Elixir's specialized formulations designed for your specific needs—their targeted plant foods eliminate the need for mixing multiple products.
