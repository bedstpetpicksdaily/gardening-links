Can I customize my Kozyard hardtop gazebo with privacy screens and awnings?

What's the difference between a hardtop gazebo and a pergola?

Hardtop gazebos feature solid roofs that provide complete weather protection and UV blocking, making them ideal for year-round use. Pergolas, like many offered by Kozyard, have open-slatted roofs that provide partial shade and a more open feel. Choose a hardtop if maximum protection is your priority.

Do Kozyard hardtop gazebos require a foundation?

Most hardtop gazebos, including Kozyard models, benefit from a concrete or permanent foundation for stability and longevity. However, some semi-permanent models can be installed on level ground with anchor kits. Check your specific model's installation guide.
