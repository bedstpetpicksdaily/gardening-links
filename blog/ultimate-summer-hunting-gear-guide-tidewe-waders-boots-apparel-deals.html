Frequently Asked Questions

What size waders should I choose from TideWe?

TideWe waders typically run slightly large. Measure your height and inseam, then compare to their sizing chart. Most customers find their true size or one size down provides the best fit. Check product reviews for specific feedback on your size range.

Are TideWe waders suitable for summer hunting?

Yes. TideWe offers breathable waders specifically designed for warm weather. These models provide waterproofing without excessive insulation, allowing proper ventilation to prevent overheating during active summer hunts.

How do I care for TideWe hunting boots to extend their lifespan?

Rinse boots with fresh water after each use, air dry away from direct heat, and apply waterproofing conditioner monthly. Store in a cool, dry place and inspect seams regularly for any damage. Proper maintenance can extend boot life significantly.

Do TideWe products come with warranties?

Most TideWe gear includes manufacturer warranties covering defects. Check your product details at checkout. Additionally, TideWe offers a satisfaction guarantee with reasonable return windows for unused items.

What's the best TideWe backpack for multi-day hunting trips?
