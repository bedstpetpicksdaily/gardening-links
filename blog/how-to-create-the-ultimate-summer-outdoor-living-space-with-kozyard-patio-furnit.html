Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a Kozyard hardtop gazebo and a pergola?

Hardtop gazebos feature solid roofs that provide complete protection from rain and intense sun, making them ideal for areas with unpredictable weather. Pergolas have open lattice roofs that allow dappled sunlight and air circulation, creating a more open feel while still offering shade. Choose based on your climate and desired aesthetic.

How do I choose the right size Kozyard shade structure for my patio?

Measure your patio space and consider how you'll use it. A 10x10 structure works well for small patios or intimate gatherings, while 12x16 or larger sizes suit expansive entertainment areas. Ensure adequate clearance around the structure for furniture placement and traffic flow.

Are Kozyard patio furniture and structures easy to maintain?

Yes, Kozyard's materials are designed for minimal maintenance. Most furniture requires occasional cleaning and light treatment, while hardtop gazebos and pergolas are built to withstand outdoor elements without regular upkeep. Always follow care instructions specific to your product's material.

Can I use Kozyard privacy screens year-round?

Absolutely. While perfect for summer, privacy screens provide year-round benefits including wind protection, reduced noise, and enhanced privacy. Their durable construction allows them to withstand various weather conditions throughout the seasons.
