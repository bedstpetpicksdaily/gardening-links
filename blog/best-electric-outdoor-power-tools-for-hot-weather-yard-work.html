Frequently Asked Questions

Are electric power tools sufficient for heavy-duty yard work?

Yes, modern electric tools like those from Giraffe Tools deliver impressive power for most residential yard work. While professional contractors may require industrial gas models, homeowners will find electric pressure washers and leaf blowers more than adequate for typical cleaning and maintenance tasks.

How long do electric tool batteries last in hot weather?

High-quality lithium-ion batteries in Giraffe Tools equipment maintain good performance in heat. Battery life varies by model and usage, but most provide 30-60 minutes of operation per charge. For extended projects, purchasing a second battery ensures continuous work.

Why are electric tools better for hot weather yard work?

Electric tools operate cooler, produce no noxious fumes, weigh less, and create less noise than gas equivalents. These factors combine to make hot weather yard work safer, more comfortable, and less exhausting when using quality electric equipment from brands like Giraffe Tools.

What pressure range should I look for in an electric pressure washer?

For residential use, 1500-2000 PSI is ideal for most cleaning tasks. Giraffe Tools offers models in this range that handle patios, decks, and vehicles effectively without risking surface damage from excessive pressure.

Can I use electric outdoor power tools on wet surfaces safely?

Modern electric tools from reputable brands like Giraffe Tools include safety features for wet environments. Always follow manufacturer guidelines, use properly grounded equipment, and never operate tools in standing water or during heavy rain.
