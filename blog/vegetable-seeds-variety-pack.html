Frequently Asked Questions

What vegetables are typically included in a variety pack?

Most vegetable seeds variety packs include a mix of popular crops like tomatoes, peppers, lettuce, carrots, beans, cucumbers, squash, and herbs. Selections vary by brand and season.

When should I plant seeds from a variety pack?

Plant seeds according to your climate zone and the specific instructions on each packet. Most cool-season crops plant in spring or fall, while warm-season vegetables plant after the last frost date.

Are heirloom seeds better than hybrid seeds?

Both have benefits: heirloom seeds produce plants you can save seeds from, while hybrids often offer disease resistance and higher yields. Choose based on your gardening preferences and goals.

Can I use variety pack seeds in containers and raised beds?

Yes, most vegetables in variety packs work well in containers and raised garden beds. Check individual packet instructions for spacing and depth requirements.
