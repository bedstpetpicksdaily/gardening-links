Frequently Asked Questions

How long do solar outdoor lights last during summer nights?

Quality solar lights typically last 8-12 hours on a single charge during summer, thanks to extended daylight hours allowing complete battery charging. Hoselink's premium solar lights are engineered for maximum runtime and consistent brightness throughout the evening.

Do solar lights work during cloudy summer days?

Yes, but with reduced efficiency. Modern solar panels, including those in Hoselink's collection, capture diffused sunlight even on cloudy days. However, battery charge may be reduced, resulting in shorter illumination periods. Plan for this when positioning lights in naturally shaded areas.

What's the best placement for maximum solar charging?

Position solar lights in areas receiving at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight daily. Avoid placing near trees, buildings, or structures that create shade. South-facing locations typically provide optimal charging throughout summer months.
