For most residential tasks like cleaning patios, decks, and driveways, a 2000-2500 PSI Giraffe Tools pressure washer is ideal. Higher PSI models are better for heavy-duty commercial applications, while lower PSI units work well for delicate surfaces like vinyl siding or painted wood.

Are Giraffe Tools electric pressure washers as powerful as gas models?

Yes, modern electric pressure washers from Giraffe Tools deliver comparable power to gas models for most residential applications. They offer the added benefits of quieter operation, zero emissions, minimal maintenance, and instant start capability without the hassle of fuel management.

Electric Giraffe Tools pressure washers require minimal maintenance compared to gas alternatives. Simply flush the system after each use, store the equipment in a dry location, and check hoses and connections periodically for wear or damage. Most models require no seasonal tune-ups or oil changes.
