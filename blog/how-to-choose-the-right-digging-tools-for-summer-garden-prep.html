Understanding Your Digging Tool Options

Before investing in digging tools, it's important to understand the different types available and their specific purposes. Spades are ideal for edging and moving soil with clean, straight cuts, while garden forks work best for loosening compacted soil and turning compost. Shovels have rounded blades perfect for scooping and moving loose materials. What's the difference between a spade and a shovel for digging?
