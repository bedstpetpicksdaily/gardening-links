Frequently Asked Questions

When should I apply summer fertilizers and plant food?

Begin fertilizing in late spring as temperatures warm, then maintain a regular feeding schedule every 2-4 weeks throughout summer. Elixir Garden Supplies' plant food products include application guides for optimal timing based on plant type.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies' weed control products safe for vegetable gardens?

Elixir offers selective herbicides designed specifically for vegetable gardens that target weeds without harming food crops. Always read product labels carefully and follow application instructions for your specific plants.

How often should I apply garden chemicals for pest control?

Application frequency depends on the specific product and pest pressure. Most treatments require application every 7-14 days during active pest seasons. Elixir's detailed product instructions provide precise guidance for maximum effectiveness.

Can I combine different Elixir garden products together?

Many products are compatible, but always check labels before mixing. Elixir Garden Supplies provides comprehensive mixing guidelines and customer support to help you create effective combined treatments safely.

What's the best way to store garden supplies throughout summer?

Store fertilizers and chemicals in cool, dry locations away from direct sunlight. Keep products in original containers with labels intact, and store away from children and pets for safety.
