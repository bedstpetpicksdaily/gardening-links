Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Gardena Aqua Cono watering globe keep plants hydrated?

The watering duration typically ranges from 7-14 days depending on soil type, plant size, temperature, and humidity levels. Sandy soils drain faster while clay soils retain moisture longer.

Can I use the Aqua Cono watering globes for outdoor gardens?

While primarily designed for potted plants, these watering globes work best for container gardens and planters. For raised beds and in-ground gardens, drip irrigation systems may be more suitable.

How do I refill the ceramic watering bulbs?

Simply remove the globe from the soil, hold it upside down, and fill with water from a pitcher or faucet. Insert back into the soil at an angle to ensure proper water distribution.

Are ceramic watering bulbs safe for all plant types?

Yes, the Gardena Aqua Cono works with most houseplants and outdoor containers. Avoid using with succulent plants and other drought-tolerant species that prefer drier conditions.
