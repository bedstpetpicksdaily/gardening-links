Top Pruning Tools for Summer 2026

Pruning is essential for maintaining plant health and encouraging new growth. When selecting pruning tools, consider both bypass and anvil cutters. Bypass pruners work like scissors and are ideal for live stems, while anvil pruners crush material against a flat edge—perfect for dead wood.
