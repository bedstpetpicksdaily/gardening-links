Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water succulent plants?

Succulents require watering only every 2-3 weeks, or when soil is completely dry. Overwatering is the most common cause of problems with succulents.

Can succulent variety packs be planted directly in raised beds?

Yes, succulents thrive in raised beds with well-draining soil. Ensure your raised garden bed has proper drainage holes and use a cactus or succulent-specific soil mix for best results.

What type of planters work best for succulents?

Use terracotta or ceramic planters with drainage holes. These materials allow soil to dry out properly between waterings, preventing root rot and other moisture-related issues.

How much sunlight do indoor succulents need?

Most succulents require 6+ hours of bright, indirect sunlight daily. Place them near south-facing or east-facing windows for optimal growth and coloration.
