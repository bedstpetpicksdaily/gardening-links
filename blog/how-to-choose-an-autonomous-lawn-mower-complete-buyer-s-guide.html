Frequently Asked Questions

How long do autonomous lawn mowers last on a single charge?

Most modern robot mowers, including ANTHBOT's models, operate for 2-4 hours per charge, covering 0.5-2 acres depending on the model and lawn conditions. They automatically return to charging docks when batteries run low.

Can autonomous lawn mowers handle rain?

Quality robotic lawn mowers like ANTHBOT's smart lawn mowers feature weather resistance and can operate in light rain. However, most models include rain sensors that pause operations during heavy downpours to protect the equipment.

Do I need to prepare my yard for an autonomous lawn mower?

Yes, you'll need to install a boundary wire around your lawn's perimeter and remove large obstacles. This setup process helps your robot mower understand lawn boundaries and prevents it from damaging garden beds or vehicles.

How does ANTHBOT compare to other autonomous lawn mower brands?

ANTHBOT stands out through superior build quality, advanced smart lawn mower technology, excellent customer support, and competitive pricing. Their robot mowers offer intelligent autonomous lawn care features that match or exceed premium competitors.

Is automatic grass cutting safe around pets and children?

Yes, modern robot mowers include safety features like obstacle detection and automatic shutdown when lifted. Still, supervise young children during operation, and most automatic grass cutting systems work best during times when pets aren't actively using the lawn.
