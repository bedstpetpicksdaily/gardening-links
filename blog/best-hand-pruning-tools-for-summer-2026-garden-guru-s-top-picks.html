Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruning shears?

Bypass shears have two curved blades that pass by each other like scissors, making them ideal for fresh, green stems and flowers. Anvil shears have one sharp blade that cuts against a flat surface, better suited for dead wood. Garden Guru Tools offers both types to match your specific pruning needs.

How often should I sharpen my pruning tools?

Most gardeners should sharpen pruning tools 2-4 times per season, depending on usage. Garden Guru Tools provides sharpening guides and replacement blades to keep your equipment in peak condition. Regular maintenance extends tool life and ensures clean cuts that promote plant health.
