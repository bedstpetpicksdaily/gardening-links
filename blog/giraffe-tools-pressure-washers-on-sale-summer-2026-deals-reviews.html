What's the difference between PSI and GPM in pressure washers?

PSI measures pressure intensity (how forcefully water hits the surface), while GPM measures flow rate (how much water is delivered per minute). Both matter: higher PSI tackles tough grime, while higher GPM covers larger areas faster. Giraffe Tools models balance both specifications for optimal cleaning efficiency.

Can I use a Giraffe Tools pressure washer on my car?

Yes, Giraffe Tools offers pressure washers with lower PSI settings (typically 1500-1800 PSI) specifically designed for vehicles. These models effectively clean cars without damaging paint when used with appropriate spray tips. Always start with lower pressure and test on a small area first.
