Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to refill the water in my AeroGarden?

Most AeroGarden models require water top-offs every 2-3 weeks depending on plant growth stage and environmental conditions. The system includes a water level indicator for convenience.

Can I use the AeroGarden to grow any type of plant?

AeroGarden works best with herbs, leafy greens, and vegetables. Specialty seed pods are available for peppers, flowers, and other plants, though some root vegetables may not perform as well in hydroponic systems.

Is the AeroGarden energy-efficient?

Yes, AeroGarden LED grow lights consume minimal electricity compared to traditional grow light systems, using approximately 20-30 watts per day depending on the model size.

What is the lifespan of AeroGarden seed pods?

Most plants thrive for 3-6 months per seed pod cycle. After harvest, you can replace the seed pod and restart the growing cycle with a fresh botanical variety.
