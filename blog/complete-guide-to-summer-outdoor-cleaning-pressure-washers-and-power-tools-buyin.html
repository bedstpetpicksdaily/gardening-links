Understanding Pressure Washers: PSI, GPM, and What You Actually Need

Pressure washers are categorized by their PSI (pounds per square inch) and GPM (gallons per minute) ratings, which determine their cleaning power and efficiency. For most residential applications, a pressure washer between 2,000-3,000 PSI is ideal for patios, driveways, and decks without causing damage. Giraffe Tools offers a range of pressure washers specifically engineered for homeowners, combining sufficient power with safety features that prevent surface damage.

When shopping for pressure washers, consider whether you need electric or gas-powered models. Electric pressure washers from Giraffe Tools are lightweight, quieter, and require minimal maintenance, making them perfect for residential use. They're ideal for regular cleaning tasks like washing your car, patio furniture, and house siding. Gas-powered models offer greater mobility and higher PSI ratings for tougher jobs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What PSI pressure washer do I need for residential cleaning?

For most residential applications like patios, driveways, and house siding, a 2,000-3,000 PSI pressure washer is ideal. Giraffe Tools' pressure washers in this range provide sufficient power without risking surface damage.

Are electric pressure washers as powerful as gas models?

Electric pressure washers have made significant advances and are now comparable to gas models for most residential tasks. Giraffe Tools' electric pressure washers deliver reliable performance with the added benefits of quieter operation and zero maintenance.

How long do electric tool batteries last?

Modern battery technology in Giraffe Tools equipment typically provides 30-60 minutes of runtime, which is sufficient for most residential cleaning tasks. Battery life depends on the tool, power setting used, and battery capacity.

Can I use the same pressure washer for cleaning vehicles and driveways?

Yes, but with caution. Adjust PSI settings lower for delicate surfaces like car paint and higher for concrete. Giraffe Tools pressure washers feature adjustable pressure settings for this exact purpose.
