Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical battery runtime on Giraffe Tools leaf blowers?

Most Giraffe Tools cordless leaf blowers deliver 20-40 minutes of runtime on a single charge, depending on the model and speed setting. Higher-capacity batteries are available for extended use on larger properties.

Are Giraffe Tools leaf blowers suitable for wet debris?

Yes, Giraffe Tools designs their leaf blowers to handle both dry and damp debris. However, heavily soaked materials may reduce efficiency. For optimal performance, use your blower when debris has partially dried.

Can I use Giraffe Tools leaf blower batteries with other outdoor equipment?

Giraffe Tools batteries are compatible across their product line, including pressure washers, snow blowers, and other electric outdoor power tools, allowing you to maximize your investment.

How do Giraffe Tools leaf blowers compare to gas-powered models?

Giraffe Tools electric blowers offer quieter operation, zero emissions, lower maintenance costs, and easier starting mechanisms. While gas models may have slightly longer unlimited runtime, Giraffe Tools' battery technology provides adequate performance for most residential needs.

What maintenance do Giraffe Tools leaf blowers require?

Electric Giraffe Tools blowers require minimal maintenance: occasional filter cleaning, battery care, and proper storage. There's no oil changes, spark plug replacement, or fuel-related upkeep.
