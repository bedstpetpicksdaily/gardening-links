Understanding Pressure Washer Specifications

Before diving into specific Giraffe Tools models, it's important to understand the key metrics that define pressure washer performance. PSI (pounds per square inch) indicates cleaning power, while GPM (gallons per minute) measures water flow rate. The combination of these two factors determines overall cleaning capability. Giraffe Tools pressure washers range from lighter-duty models perfect for delicate surfaces to heavy-duty options for tough industrial cleaning tasks. Most homeowners find that 2000-3000 PSI provides excellent cleaning power for residential applications without risking damage to painted surfaces or siding.

Frequently Asked Questions

What PSI do I need for residential cleaning?

Most residential cleaning tasks are handled effectively by pressure washers in the 2000-3000 PSI range. Giraffe Tools offers models within this sweet spot that provide excellent cleaning power without risking damage to common residential surfaces like vinyl siding, painted wood, and composite decking.

Are Giraffe Tools pressure washers suitable for professional contractors?

Yes, Giraffe Tools offers commercial-grade pressure washer models designed to handle professional workloads. These heavy-duty options provide higher PSI ratings, increased water flow, and enhanced durability for frequent, demanding cleaning applications.

How do electric pressure washers compare to gas models?

Giraffe Tools electric pressure washers are quieter, require less maintenance, produce zero emissions, and are more environmentally friendly than gas alternatives. While they typically offer slightly lower PSI than high-end gas models, they're perfect for most residential and light commercial work at a more affordable price point.

Giraffe Tools pressure washers can clean patios, decks, driveways, fences, siding, vehicles, and garden furniture. Always start with lower PSI settings on delicate surfaces and test inconspicuous areas first to prevent damage.
