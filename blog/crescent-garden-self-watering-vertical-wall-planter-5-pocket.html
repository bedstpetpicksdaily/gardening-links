Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you actually need to refill the water reservoir?

In July heat with full sun exposure, expect to refill every 7-10 days depending on plant type and soil moisture. Herbs dry faster than succulents. If you're away for two weeks, add a soaker hose backup or pair this with a basic timer system—the planter alone won't sustain plants that long.

Can you grow vegetables or just ornamental plants?

Both work, but choose carefully by pocket. Top pockets are too exposed for heavy water-lovers like tomatoes. Use them for herbs, strawberries, or ornamentals. Bottom pockets handle lettuce and lighter vegetables better. Mixing plant types on one planter complicates watering schedules.

Is assembly as bad as some reviews say?

Yes, it's tedious. Budget 20-30 minutes per unit and have clips laid out before starting. Most frustration comes from the snap-lock system not clicking properly on the first try. Don't rush—loose clips create leaks that void the self-watering advantage. Watch a YouTube assembly video first.
