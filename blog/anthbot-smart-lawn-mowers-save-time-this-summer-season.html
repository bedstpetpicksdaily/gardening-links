Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for an ANTHBOT robot mower to cut my entire lawn?

ANTHBOT smart lawn mowers work efficiently based on your lawn size and cutting schedule. Most robotic lawn mowers from ANTHBOT complete typical residential lawns (up to 1 acre) within a few hours, though they often work in multiple sessions throughout the week for optimal grass health.

Can ANTHBOT automatic grass cutting systems handle slopes and uneven terrain?

Yes, ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers are engineered to navigate slopes up to specific angles (typically 20-35 degrees depending on the model). The intelligent autonomous lawn care system adjusts cutting patterns to handle varying terrain while maintaining consistent grass length.

Do I need WiFi for ANTHBOT smart lawn mowers to work?

While WiFi enables remote monitoring and advanced features through the mobile app, ANTHBOT's robot mowers can operate autonomously without WiFi using their pre-programmed schedules. WiFi access simply allows you to control, monitor, and adjust your mower from anywhere.

How often should I maintain my ANTHBOT robotic lawn mower?

ANTHBOT smart lawn mowers require minimal maintenance—mainly cleaning the cutting blades and chassis regularly, checking the boundary wire, and keeping the charging dock clear. Most owners find maintenance takes just 15-20 minutes monthly.

Will an ANTHBOT robot mower work in rain?

Most ANTHBOT automatic grass cutting systems are weather-resistant and can operate in light rain. However, extremely heavy rainfall may trigger automatic return-to-dock functions. Check your specific model's weather specifications for detailed information.
