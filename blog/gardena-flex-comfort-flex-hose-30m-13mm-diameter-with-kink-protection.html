Frequently Asked Questions

Does the kink protection actually prevent all kinks?

No hose prevents every possible kink—nothing physically does. What Gardena's design does is resist the permanent creasing that damages cheaper hoses. Even when sharply bent, the reinforced structure bounces back. I noticed after a week of use that sections would recover their shape overnight, whereas my old hose stayed permanently collapsed in kinked spots.

Is 30m too long for a small garden?

It depends on your layout and water source location. Measure from your spigot to your farthest garden zone—if that's under 15 meters, a 20m would work fine. The 30m makes sense if your water source is at your house corner and your beds extend across a larger yard, or if you want flexibility to reach multiple zones without disconnecting.

How does water pressure compare to standard hoses?

The 13mm diameter maintains solid pressure for hand watering and soaker hose use. Where you notice it less is with high-flow spray nozzles—you won't get the aggressive pressure of a 16mm hose, but for most garden tasks in July heat, you're watering carefully anyway to avoid evaporation, so the moderate pressure is actually a feature, not a bug.
