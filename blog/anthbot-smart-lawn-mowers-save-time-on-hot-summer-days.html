ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers require minimal maintenance compared to traditional mowers. Regular blade checks, occasional base station cleaning, and seasonal inspections are typically all that's needed. Refer to your specific model's manual for detailed maintenance schedules.

Can ANTHBOT robot mowers handle uneven terrain?

Yes, ANTHBOT autonomous lawn care systems are designed to navigate various terrain types, including slight slopes and uneven grass. However, extremely steep hills or dense obstacles may affect performance. Check your specific model's specifications for terrain compatibility.

Do ANTHBOT automatic grass cutting mowers work in rain?

Most ANTHBOT smart lawn mowers are weather-resistant and can operate in light rain. However, heavy rainfall may trigger safety shutdowns. Your mower can be programmed to avoid mowing during severe weather, or it will automatically return to its base station for protection.

How long does the battery last on ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers?

ANTHBOT robot mower battery life varies by model and yard size but typically ranges from 60-120 minutes of mowing per charge. Most models automatically dock for charging and resume their schedule afterward, allowing all-day coverage.

Is setup complicated for ANTHBOT smart lawn mowers?

Initial setup involves creating a boundary perimeter and configuring your mowing schedule through the companion app. While this takes some time initially, it's a one-time process. ANTHBOT provides detailed setup guides to make the process straightforward.
