Frequently Asked Questions

How long do self-watering planters keep plants hydrated?

Most self-watering planters can sustain plants for 2-4 weeks depending on pot size, plant type, and environmental conditions. Larger reservoirs provide longer watering intervals.

Are self-watering pots suitable for all types of plants?

Self-watering pots work well for most plants, but succulents and cacti prefer drier conditions. Check individual plant water requirements before using self-watering containers.

Can self-watering planters be used indoors and outdoors?

Yes, self-watering planters are versatile and work effectively both indoors for houseplants and outdoors for flowers, vegetables, and herbs.

What size self-watering planter do I need?

Choose planter size based on plant mature size and root system. Small pots (4-6 inches) suit herbs and small plants, while large pots (12+ inches) work for vegetables and shrubs.
