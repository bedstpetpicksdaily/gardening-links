Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I fertilize during summer?

Most summer plants benefit from weekly or bi-weekly feeding, depending on growth stage and plant type. Elixir Garden Supplies recommends adjusting frequency based on your specific plant's needs and soil conditions. Vegetables typically need more frequent feeding than ornamentals.

Can I use winter fertilizers in summer?

Not ideally. Winter fertilizers have different nutrient ratios designed for dormancy and slower growth. Summer-specific formulas like those from Elixir Garden Supplies are optimized for the rapid growth and higher nutrient demands of the warm season.

What's the best time to apply summer fertilizer?

Apply fertilizers in early morning or late evening to prevent leaf burn in intense midday heat. Always water plants thoroughly before application. Elixir Garden Supplies' product instructions provide specific timing recommendations for their various formulations.

Are organic or synthetic fertilizers better for summer gardening?

Both have advantages. Synthetic fertilizers from Elixir Garden Supplies work faster in summer heat, while organic options build soil health long-term. Choose based on your gardening philosophy and immediate plant needs.

How do I prevent fertilizer burn in hot weather?

Always follow dilution rates precisely, water plants before fertilizing, apply in cooler parts of the day, and avoid applying during extreme heat waves. Elixir Garden Supplies formulas are designed with built-in safeguards against burn when used correctly.
