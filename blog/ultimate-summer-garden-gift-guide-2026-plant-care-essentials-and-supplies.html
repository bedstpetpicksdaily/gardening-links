What's the best way to apply liquid vs. granular fertilizers?

Liquid fertilizers offer quick nutrient availability and even distribution, ideal for container plants. Granular options provide slow-release nutrition perfect for established beds. Elixir's product descriptions clarify best application methods for each formulation.
