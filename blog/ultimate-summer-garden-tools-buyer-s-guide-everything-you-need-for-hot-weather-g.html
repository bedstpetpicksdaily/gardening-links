Frequently Asked Questions

What size Hoselink retractable hose reel do I need for my summer garden?

Hoselink offers various reel sizes from 15m to 50m options. Measure your garden space and identify your watering points to determine the ideal length. A 30m reel typically covers most residential gardens, while larger properties may benefit from 50m models or multiple strategically placed reels.

How often should I water my garden during Australian summers?

During peak summer heat (35°C+), most gardens need daily watering, preferably early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation. Hoselink's efficient watering systems help deliver water directly to plant roots, reducing water loss and ensuring consistent hydration even during water restrictions.
