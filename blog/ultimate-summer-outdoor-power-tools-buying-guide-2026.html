Frequently Asked Questions

What PSI do I need for different outdoor cleaning tasks?

For gentle surfaces like deck and furniture, 1300-1500 PSI is sufficient. Driveways and patios typically need 2000-2800 PSI, while tough concrete cleaning may require 3000+ PSI. Giraffe Tools pressure washers offer adjustable settings to accommodate various surfaces.

How long do batteries last on electric outdoor power tools?

Modern cordless tools from quality brands like Giraffe Tools typically provide 20-60 minutes of runtime depending on the model and workload. For larger projects, purchasing additional batteries ensures continuous work without extended downtime.

Are electric outdoor power tools as powerful as gas-powered options?

Yes, modern electric tools perform comparably to gas-powered equipment. Giraffe Tools' electric pressure washers and leaf blowers deliver impressive power ratings while offering advantages like zero emissions, quieter operation, and lower maintenance requirements.

What maintenance do Giraffe Tools outdoor power tools require?

Electric tools require minimal maintenance—primarily keeping them clean and dry between uses. Unlike gas equipment, there's no fuel stabilization, spark plug replacement, or seasonal tune-ups needed. Battery care involves charging after use and proper storage.

Can I use the same pressure washer for all outdoor surfaces?

Most quality pressure washers like those from Giraffe Tools feature adjustable nozzles and pressure settings. This allows safe use on various surfaces from delicate wood to tough concrete, though you should always follow manufacturer guidelines for specific materials.
