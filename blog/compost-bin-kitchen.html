Frequently Asked Questions

What materials can I compost in a kitchen bin?

Fruit and vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, eggshells, and paper products work best. Avoid meat, dairy, and oils to prevent odors.

How often should I empty my kitchen compost bin?

Empty your bin weekly or when it reaches capacity to prevent odors and pest issues, then add the contents to your garden beds or raised beds.

Can kitchen compost bins work with raised garden beds?

Absolutely! Kitchen compost provides excellent soil amendments for raised beds, planters, and outdoor plant growth.

Do kitchen compost bins produce finished compost?

Kitchen bins collect materials for composting. Transfer contents to outdoor piles or bins where they'll fully decompose into finished compost.
