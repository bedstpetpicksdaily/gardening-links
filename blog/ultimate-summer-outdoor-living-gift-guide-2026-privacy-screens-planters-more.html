Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a gazebo and a pergola?

Gazebos, especially Kozyard's hardtop models, provide full roof coverage and maximum protection from sun and rain. Pergolas offer a latticed or slatted roof design that allows filtered light and air circulation while still providing partial shade and an elegant architectural element.

How do Kozyard privacy screens improve outdoor living?

Kozyard privacy screens create secluded, intimate spaces by blocking views and reducing wind. They're perfect for creating distinct zones in your garden, hiding utilities, or simply enhancing your sense of privacy and comfort in outdoor entertaining areas.

Are Kozyard outdoor furniture and planters weather-resistant?

Yes, Kozyard specializes in weather-resistant outdoor products designed to withstand sun, rain, and temperature fluctuations. Their materials are selected for durability and minimal maintenance, ensuring your investment looks beautiful for years.

Can I install a Kozyard gazebo or pergola myself?

While some smaller components can be DIY projects, most hardtop gazebos and pergolas benefit from professional installation to ensure proper structural integrity and warranty compliance. Kozyard offers installation guidance and can connect you with certified installers.

What size privacy screen or planter should I choose?

Consider your space dimensions, existing décor, and specific needs. For privacy screens, measure the area you want to screen. For planters, think about sight lines and the plants you'll grow. Kozyard offers various sizes to suit different garden scales.
