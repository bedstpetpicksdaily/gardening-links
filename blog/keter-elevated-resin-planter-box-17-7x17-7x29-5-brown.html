It resists rotation and mildew better than wood, but UV degradation is real. The plastic will fade after 3-4 seasons of direct afternoon sun, and cold climates can make it brittle around year 5-7. If you're in a hot climate, expect noticeable color fading by year two. It's not going to fail, but it won't look new for long. Using a weatherproof cover in winter extends life significantly.

Can I grow a full season of vegetables in this size, or is it just decorative?

You can grow herbs, lettuce, or shallow-rooted plants successfully.
