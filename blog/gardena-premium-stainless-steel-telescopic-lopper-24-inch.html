Go thicker than that and you'll feel resistance, but the bypass design means it still cuts rather than crushes. For anything over 2 inches, switch to a pruning saw.

Will the stainless steel actually resist rust in humid climates?

Carbon steel tools we tested showed surface rust in the same conditions. Rinse it after use in wet conditions and store it dry, and you'll have decades of life from this tool.
