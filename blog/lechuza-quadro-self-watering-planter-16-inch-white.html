Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Lechuza Quadro self-watering system work?

The planter features a water reservoir at the base with a fill indicator. Plants draw water through a specialized substrate as needed, creating an automated watering cycle that prevents both underwatering and overwatering.

What size plants can I grow in the 16-inch Lechuza Quadro?

The 16-inch capacity accommodates medium to large plants, including ornamental shrubs, perennials, herbs, and vegetable plants. It's suitable for single specimens or multiple smaller plants.

Can I use the Lechuza Quadro outdoors year-round?

Yes, the weather-resistant plastic construction withstands outdoor conditions. However, in freezing climates, drain the water reservoir before winter to prevent ice expansion damage.

How often should I refill the water reservoir?

Refill frequency depends on plant type, season, and climate. The built-in water level indicator shows when refilling is needed, typically every 1-3 weeks during growing season.
