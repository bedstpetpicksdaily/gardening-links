Frequently Asked Questions

What pressure washer PSI is best for summer patio and deck cleaning?

For most patios and decks, 1500-2000 PSI is ideal. Giraffe Tools pressure washers offer adjustable settings to prevent damage to delicate surfaces while still removing tough stains effectively.

Can Giraffe Tools leaf blowers handle wet debris and leaves?

Giraffe Tools leaf blowers work best with dry debris. For wet leaves and grass clippings, it's better to wait for dry conditions or use the equipment to clear paths while debris dries.

How long do Giraffe Tools cordless batteries typically last?

Battery runtime depends on the specific model and usage intensity. Most Giraffe Tools cordless equipment provides 30-60 minutes of operation per charge, with quick-recharge options available.

Is Giraffe Tools equipment suitable for small apartment patios?

Yes, Giraffe Tools offers compact, lightweight models perfect for apartments and small outdoor spaces. Their electric design is quieter than gas alternatives, ideal for shared living situations.

What safety precautions should I take when using Giraffe Tools pressure washers?

Always wear protective eyewear and gloves, keep the nozzle pointed away from people and pets, and avoid spraying electrical outlets or fixtures. Start with lower pressure settings on unfamiliar surfaces.
