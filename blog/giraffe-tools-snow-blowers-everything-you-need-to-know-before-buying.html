Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Giraffe Tools snow blower battery last on a single charge?

Battery runtime varies by model and snow conditions, typically ranging from 30 to 60 minutes of continuous operation. Lighter snow and slower clearing speeds can extend runtime significantly, while heavy, wet snow may reduce it. Most models allow for battery swapping to extend total clearing time.

Can Giraffe Tools snow blowers handle wet, heavy snow?

Yes, Giraffe Tools snow blowers are engineered to handle various snow types, including wet, heavy accumulation. The electric motors provide consistent torque without power loss, making them effective across different conditions. For optimal performance with heavy snow, clear in multiple passes rather than single deep passes.

What's the warranty on Giraffe Tools snow blowers?

Giraffe Tools provides comprehensive warranty coverage on their snow blowers. Coverage typically includes the motor, housing, and battery systems. Visit Giraffe Tools' website for specific warranty details on your chosen model, as coverage may vary.

Are Giraffe Tools snow blowers suitable for commercial use?

Giraffe Tools snow blowers are primarily designed for residential properties and smaller commercial applications. For heavy-duty commercial snow removal requiring extended daily use, consult Giraffe Tools directly about commercial-grade options or rental programs.

How do I store a Giraffe Tools snow blower during off-season?

Electric snow blowers require minimal storage preparation. Simply clean the unit, remove the battery if desired, and store in a dry location. Unlike gas equipment, there's no need for fuel stabilizer or engine winterization, making storage significantly simpler.
