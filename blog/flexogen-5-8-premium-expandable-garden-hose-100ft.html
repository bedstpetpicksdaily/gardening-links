Frequently Asked Questions

Does this hose really stay kink-free, or is that marketing hype?

Partially true. The expandable design resists kinks better than standard rubber, but "kink-free" oversells it. Tight coils and bent angles still cause temporary kinks. The key is proper storage and handling. Coil it loosely, and kinks minimize significantly. Coil it tight, and you'll fight with them.

Will it handle high-pressure nozzles or just gentle watering?

It handles moderate pressure well—standard spray nozzles, soaker hose adapters, and multi-pattern nozzles work without issue. Power washers or high-PSI attachments? That's pushing it. The expandable design isn't rated for industrial pressure, so stick to garden-grade nozzles.

How long does the 100-foot length actually last before leaks develop?

Based on the 4.3-star rating with 500+ reviews, most users report 2-3 seasons of regular use before noticeable wear. Storage matters: bring it inside during winter rather than leaving it coiled in the shed, and lifespan extends significantly. July heat accelerates degradation if left exposed, so store it indoors when not in use.
