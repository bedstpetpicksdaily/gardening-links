Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I apply plant food during summer?

Most Elixir Garden Supplies plant food products recommend feeding every 2-3 weeks during the active growing season. However, container plants and heavy feeders like tomatoes may benefit from weekly applications. Always follow the specific instructions on your chosen product for best results.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies fertilizers safe for vegetable gardens?

Yes, Elixir Garden Supplies offers fertilizers specifically formulated for edible crops. Always choose products labelled for vegetable use and follow recommended application rates. Their organic options are ideal if you prefer natural fertilizers for food production.

When is the best time to apply weed control from Elixir?

Early morning or late evening applications are most effective, as weeds are less stressed by heat. Avoid application during extreme heat or if rain is forecast within 24 hours. Always read product instructions carefully for optimal timing and conditions.

What's the difference between selective and non-selective herbicides?

Selective herbicides target specific weed types without harming desirable plants, making them ideal for lawns. Non-selective herbicides kill most vegetation and are best for pathways and patios. Elixir Garden Supplies clearly labels products so you can choose appropriately for your garden areas.

Can I use garden chemicals safely around children and pets?

Many Elixir Garden Supplies products are pet and child-friendly when used as directed. Always store chemicals safely, follow label instructions exactly, and keep users away from treated areas during application. Consider organic alternatives for high-traffic family garden spaces.
