Frequently Asked Questions

How many mosquito dunks do I need for my pond or water feature?

Each dunk treats up to 100 square feet of surface water for up to 30 days, so a 6-pack provides coverage for approximately 600 square feet total or can treat a smaller area multiple times throughout the season. For best results, break the dunk into quarters and distribute evenly across your water surface rather than using it all in one spot.

Are Mosquito Dunks safe to use around fish and plants?

Yes, Mosquito Dunks are completely safe for fish, aquatic plants, birds, and beneficial insects because they contain Bti (Bacillus thuringiensis israelensis), which only targets mosquito and midge larvae. They won't harm your pond ecosystem or damage water plants when used as directed.

How long do Mosquito Dunks last and when should I reapply them?

Each dunk remains effective for approximately 30 days before needing replacement, though heavy rain or water circulation may reduce this timeframe. For continuous mosquito control throughout the season, plan to replace dunks monthly or follow the package instructions based on your specific water conditions.

Will Mosquito Dunks work in moving water or do they only work in still water?

Mosquito Dunks work best in still or stagnant water like ponds, birdbaths, rain barrels, and flower pots where larvae develop, but they're less effective in moving water like fountains or streams. If you have a fountain or waterfall, focus placement in calmer pool areas where mosquito larvae are most likely to breed.
