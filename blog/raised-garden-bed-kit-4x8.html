Frequently Asked Questions

What soil depth do I need for a 4x8 raised garden bed?

Most vegetables require 12 inches of soil minimum, though 18 inches is ideal for root crops like carrots and potatoes. A 4x8 bed at 12 inches deep requires approximately 32 cubic feet of soil.

How much does it cost to fill a 4x8 raised garden bed?

Fill cost varies by location and soil quality. Budget $75-150 for quality garden soil to fill a 12-inch deep 4x8 bed, or $150-250 for premium organic mixes.

Can I use a 4x8 raised bed for flowers as well as vegetables?

Yes, 4x8 beds work excellently for ornamental flowers, perennials, and mixed plantings. The larger space allows creative garden designs combining vegetables, herbs, and decorative plants.

How long do metal raised garden beds last?

Quality metal beds with proper coatings typically last 10-15 years or longer. Galvanized steel and powder-coated metals offer superior rust resistance compared to untreated options.
