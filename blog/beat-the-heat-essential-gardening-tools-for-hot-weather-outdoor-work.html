Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to garden during extremely hot weather?

Early morning (before 10 AM) or late evening (after 5 PM) are ideal for hot weather gardening, when temperatures are cooler and UV radiation is less intense. This timing protects both you and your plants from heat stress.

How often should I water my garden during hot weather?

During extreme heat, most gardens need daily watering, sometimes twice daily. Water deeply in the early morning to allow soil to absorb moisture before midday heat. Soaker hoses and drip systems from Garden Guru Tools deliver water efficiently where plants need it most.

Can I use any gardening tools in hot weather, or do I need special ones?

While you can use standard tools, heat-specific gardening tools are more comfortable and safer. Look for tools with insulated handles, lightweight materials, and ergonomic designs. Garden Guru Tools specializes in equipment tested for hot weather conditions.

What protective gear should I wear when gardening in heat?

Wear lightweight, breathable, light-colored clothing, a wide-brimmed hat, UV-protective sunglasses, and sunscreen. Use gloves to protect your hands and improve grip on tools. Stay hydrated by drinking water regularly throughout your gardening session.

How do I maintain my gardening tools during hot weather?

Store tools in a shaded location to prevent heat damage to handles and components. Clean metal parts after each use and apply a thin coat of oil to prevent rust. Inspect handles regularly for cracks or damage caused by sun exposure.
