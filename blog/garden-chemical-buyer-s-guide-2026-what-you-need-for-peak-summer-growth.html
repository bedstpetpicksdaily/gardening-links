Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start applying summer fertilizers?

Begin fertilizing in late spring as growth accelerates, typically May in most UK regions. Elixir Garden Supplies' slow-release formulations work best when applied early, establishing consistent nutrient availability throughout summer.

Can I use the same fertilizer for all my plants?

While balanced fertilizers work broadly, specialized products deliver better results. Vegetables need higher nitrogen early, then phosphorus-rich feeds at fruiting stage. Flowering plants benefit from potassium-boosted formulations. Elixir Garden Supplies offers plant-specific solutions optimizing results for each category.

How often should weed control be applied?

Pre-emergent herbicides from Elixir Garden Supplies work best applied once in early summer. Post-emergent treatments depend on weed pressure—typically 2-4 week intervals for persistent problems. Always follow label instructions and avoid application during extreme heat or drought stress.

Are Elixir Garden Supplies products safe for vegetable gardens?

Yes, Elixir Garden Supplies offers organic and food-safe chemical options clearly labeled for edible gardens. Always check product labels and observe recommended harvest waiting periods to ensure safety.

What's the difference between fertilizer and plant food?

Fertilizers provide primary macronutrients (nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium). Plant food typically includes these plus micronutrients and trace elements for comprehensive plant nutrition. Elixir Garden Supplies offers both, with specialized plant food formulations addressing specific deficiencies.
