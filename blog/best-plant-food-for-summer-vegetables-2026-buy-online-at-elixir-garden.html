What NPK ratio is best for summer vegetables?

For leafy vegetables, use a higher nitrogen ratio (e.g., 10-5-5). For fruiting crops like tomatoes and peppers, choose a formulation with higher potassium (e.g., 5-5-10). Elixir Garden Supplies stocks products for both types—check product labels for specific recommendations.

Most summer vegetables benefit from weekly or fortnightly feeding once flowering begins. Liquid plant foods from Elixir Garden are ideal for weekly applications, while granular products offer longer-lasting nutrition with less frequent application needed.
