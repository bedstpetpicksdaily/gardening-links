Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to apply Elixir fertilizers during summer?

Early morning or late evening applications work best to minimize stress on plants. Follow package instructions for frequency, typically every 2-4 weeks depending on plant type and growth stage.

Are Elixir's weed control products safe for vegetable gardens?

Elixir offers both selective and non-selective weed control solutions. For vegetable gardens, choose their targeted products designed specifically for edible plants, and always follow label directions carefully.

Can I use multiple Elixir products together?

Yes, Elixir products are formulated to work together safely. However, always read labels and avoid mixing concentrated chemicals without guidance. When in doubt, contact Elixir's customer support for personalized recommendations.

How long do Elixir garden supplies last once opened?

Most Elixir products maintain effectiveness for the entire growing season if stored in a cool, dry place. Liquid fertilizers typically last 1-2 seasons, while granular products can remain viable for 2+ years with proper storage.
