What makes TideWe waders better for summer than traditional neoprene?

TideWe's breathable waders use advanced synthetic materials that allow moisture vapor to escape while blocking water penetration. This prevents the heat-trapping effect of neoprene, keeping you significantly cooler during warm-weather hunting without sacrificing waterproofing or durability.

Can TideWe hunting apparel be used for other outdoor activities?

Absolutely. TideWe's outdoor apparel is designed with versatility in mind. The moisture-wicking, breathable materials and durable construction work well for yard work, gardening, fishing, camping, and other outdoor pursuits beyond hunting.

How do I care for TideWe breathable waders to maintain waterproofing?

Rinse your TideWe waders with fresh water after each use to remove salt, mud, and debris. Allow them to air dry completely in a cool location away from direct heat. Periodically apply appropriate fabric treatments to maintain the water-repellent coating on breathable materials.
