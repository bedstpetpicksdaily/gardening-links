Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an autonomous lawn mower battery last?

Most quality robot mowers like ANTHBOT models run 2-4 hours per charge, automatically returning to their charging dock when battery runs low. Larger properties may require extended runtime models that recharge and resume cutting throughout the day.

Can autonomous lawn mowers handle rain and wet grass?

ANTHBOT's robotic lawn mowers feature weather sensors that pause operation during heavy rain, protecting both the mower and your lawn. However, they can handle light moisture and damp grass without issues, resuming automatically once conditions improve.

Do I need to install a boundary wire for smart lawn mowers?

Most ANTHBOT autonomous lawn care systems use GPS technology combined with optional boundary wires for added precision. GPS-only models work great for open properties, while combination systems provide maximum control for complex yard layouts with multiple zones.

How much maintenance do automatic grass cutting systems require?

ANTHBOT robot mowers require minimal maintenance—occasional blade sharpening, debris cleaning, and annual check-ups. They're far less demanding than traditional mowers, with no oil changes, spark plug replacements, or seasonal tune-ups needed.

Are autonomous lawn mowers safe around children and pets?

Yes, ANTHBOT robotic lawn mowers include safety cutoff features, obstacle detection, and quiet operation. However, supervision is recommended, and you should schedule mowing during times when pets and children aren't actively using the yard.
