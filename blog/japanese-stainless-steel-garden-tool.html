Frequently Asked Questions

Are Japanese stainless steel garden tools worth the investment?

Yes, Japanese tools offer superior edge retention, durability, and ergonomic design that reduce fatigue and improve gardening efficiency, making them worthwhile for regular gardeners.

How do I maintain Japanese stainless steel garden tools?

Clean tools after each use, dry thoroughly, store in a dry location, and sharpen blades periodically with a whetstone to maintain optimal performance.

What tools should I include in a basic Japanese garden set?

Essential tools include a hand hoe for weeding, pruning shears for trimming, a spade for digging, and a cultivator for soil preparation in raised beds and planters.

Can Japanese garden tools rust?

While stainless steel resists rust, it's not completely rust-proof. Proper drying and storage in dry conditions prevent any potential corrosion.
