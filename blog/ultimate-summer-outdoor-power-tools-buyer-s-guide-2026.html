Frequently Asked Questions

Are electric outdoor power tools as powerful as gas models?

Modern electric tools, including Giraffe Tools' lineup, deliver comparable or superior power to gas equivalents. Advances in battery technology provide consistent power output, and many users find electric pressure washers and leaf blowers handle residential tasks better while being quieter and easier to maintain.

How long do batteries last on electric power tools?

Battery runtime varies by model and usage intensity, typically ranging from 20-60 minutes per charge. Giraffe Tools designs their electric equipment for efficient power consumption, allowing most summer projects to be completed on a single charge. Having backup batteries ensures uninterrupted work.

What PSI pressure washer do I need for my driveway?

For driveway cleaning, a pressure washer between 2500-3000 PSI is ideal. Giraffe Tools offers models in this range that effectively remove dirt, algae, and stains without damaging concrete. Always use lower PSI on delicate surfaces like painted wood or composite decking.

Can I use one battery for multiple Giraffe Tools products?

Yes, Giraffe Tools designs many products around compatible battery platforms. This approach reduces overall investment and storage needs, making it economical to build a complete outdoor power tool collection.

How often should I maintain my electric pressure washer?

Electric pressure washers require minimal maintenance compared to gas models. After each use, flush the system with clean water, dry the nozzle, and store in a cool, dry location. Check battery contacts periodically and follow manufacturer guidelines for seasonal storage.
