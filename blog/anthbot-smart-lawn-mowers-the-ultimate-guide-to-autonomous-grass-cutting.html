Simply set your preferences through the mobile app, and your ANTHBOT mower gets to work—handling the cutting while you relax indoors or focus on other tasks.

The autonomous nature of ANTHBOT's technology means your mower operates on a predetermined schedule, cutting grass frequently in small increments. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an ANTHBOT smart mower take to charge?

Most ANTHBOT models charge fully in 60-90 minutes, providing 2-4 hours of continuous mowing per charge. The robotic mower automatically returns to its dock when the battery drops below a certain threshold.

Can ANTHBOT mowers handle hills and uneven terrain?

Yes, ANTHBOT robotic mowers are engineered to handle slopes up to 35 degrees and various terrain types. Their advanced traction and weight distribution systems ensure stable operation on challenging landscapes.

Is it necessary to remove obstacles from my yard before using ANTHBOT?

While ANTHBOT mowers can navigate around obstacles, clearing your yard of toys, furniture, and debris improves efficiency and prevents potential entanglement. The smart sensors will detect larger obstacles and navigate accordingly.

ANTHBOT smart mowers are weather-resistant and can operate in light rain. However, they'll automatically return to the charging dock during heavy rain to prevent damage and ensure optimal performance.
