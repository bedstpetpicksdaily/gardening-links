Early morning or late evening application is ideal to minimize evaporation and allow plants to absorb nutrients when temperatures are cooler. Most Elixir products work best when soil is moist, so water before applying dry formulations.

Elixir offers both general-use and vegetable-safe formulations. Always check product labels to ensure your chosen weed killer is approved for use near edible plants. Their organic options are particularly safe for vegetable gardens.
