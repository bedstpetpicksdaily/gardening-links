Is ANTHBOT suitable for small yards?

Yes. While ANTHBOT robot mowers excel on medium to large lawns (up to 15,000+ sq ft), they offer models specifically designed for smaller properties. Their compact design and intelligent navigation make them adaptable to various yard sizes and complex layouts.

How do I install an ANTHBOT robotic lawn mower?

ANTHBOT mowers require boundary wire installation to define cutting areas—typically a 1-2 hour DIY process. The wire marks garden edges and no-go zones. Once installed, the robot uses GPS and sensors to navigate autonomously. ANTHBOT provides detailed installation guides and customer support to simplify setup.

ANTHBOT robot mowers are weather-resistant and can operate in light rain. However, they feature automatic weather detection that pauses cutting during heavy downpours and reschedules sessions for optimal conditions. This protects your lawn and extends mower lifespan.
