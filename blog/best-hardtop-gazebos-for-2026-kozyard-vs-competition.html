Frequently Asked Questions

Are hardtop gazebos better than fabric ones?

Hardtop gazebos offer superior durability and weather resistance compared to fabric alternatives. While fabric gazebos are more affordable, hardtop options like Kozyard's provide better long-term value, withstanding snow load, intense UV rays, and severe wind. They're ideal for permanent outdoor structures.

How long do Kozyard hardtop gazebos last?

With proper maintenance, Kozyard hardtop gazebos typically last 15-25+ years. Their aluminum frames resist rust, and high-quality polycarbonate or metal roofs are engineered for longevity. Regular cleaning and minor maintenance will maximize your gazebo's lifespan.

Can I customize my Kozyard gazebo?

Yes! Kozyard offers multiple sizes, finishes, and styles to customize your gazebo. Additionally, you can enhance your space with their compatible pergolas, privacy screens, awnings, and planters to create a fully coordinated outdoor living area.
