Frequently Asked Questions

Does this sensor work with smart irrigation systems, or just for monitoring?

The SmartSensor monitors soil moisture and sends app notifications, but it doesn't automatically trigger most irrigation systems without additional integration hardware. You'll receive alerts to water manually, or you can pair it with compatible Gardena smart watering controllers for automated irrigation based on real soil data. Check compatibility with your specific irrigation setup before buying.

How deep does the sensor probe actually measure, and does it work in containers?

The probe reaches approximately 10-15cm (4-6 inches) into soil, capturing moisture at the root zone depth where most plants actually draw water. It works effectively in containers, raised beds, and in-ground gardens. However, very shallow containers (under 6 inches) won't give you a meaningful reading because the probe bottoms out above the root layer.

What happens if the WiFi drops for days—does the sensor store data locally?

The sensor does not have local data logging. If WiFi connection drops for extended periods, you lose real-time notifications during that window. However, the sensor itself continues measuring soil moisture; you'll see historical data once connectivity restores. This is a genuine limitation if you have unreliable internet, and you should test your garden's WiFi strength before purchasing.
