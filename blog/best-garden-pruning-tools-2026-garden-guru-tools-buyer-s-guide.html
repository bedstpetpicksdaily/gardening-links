Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bypass and anvil pruners?

Bypass pruners have two curved blades that slide past each other like scissors, ideal for clean cuts on living plants. Anvil pruners have one sharp blade that cuts against a flat surface, better for dead wood. Garden Guru Tools offers both types for different applications.

How often should I sharpen my pruning tools?

Most pruning tools should be sharpened 1-2 times per gardening season, depending on use frequency. Garden Guru Tools provides sharpening guides and replacement blade options to keep your tools performing optimally.

Are Garden Guru Tools suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. Garden Guru Tools offers beginner-friendly options with clear instructions and responsive customer support. Their ergonomic designs make them comfortable for users of all experience levels.

What cutting capacity do I need for my plants?

For flowers and small branches up to 1/4 inch, use bypass pruners. For branches 1/4 to 1 inch thick, use loppers. For branches over 1 inch, use pruning saws. Garden Guru Tools offers tools for all these ranges.
