Frequently Asked Questions

What's the weight capacity of garden kneelers?

Most quality garden kneelers and seats support between 250-300 pounds. Always check the manufacturer's specifications before purchasing to ensure it meets your needs.

Can I use a garden kneeler on uneven ground?

While some kneelers work on uneven surfaces, they perform best on relatively flat ground. Consider the terrain of your garden beds and raised garden areas when selecting a model.

Are garden kneelers easy to store?

Most garden kneelers are lightweight and foldable, making them easy to store in sheds or garages. Some models are more compact than others, so choose based on your available storage space.

How do handles help with gardening?

Handles provide crucial support when transitioning from kneeling to standing, reducing stress on knees and back. They're especially helpful for older gardeners or those with mobility concerns.
