Frequently Asked Questions

What size plants can I grow in 5-gallon fabric pots?

5-gallon fabric pots work well for most plants, including tomatoes, peppers, herbs, lettuce, and smaller ornamental plants. They're ideal for container gardening and temporary plantings.

How long do 5-gallon fabric pots last?

Quality fabric pots typically last 3-5 years or more with proper care. Store them in a cool, dry place during off-seasons to extend their lifespan.

Do I need drainage holes in fabric pots?

No, most 5-gallon fabric pots feature built-in drainage throughout the fabric material itself, eliminating the need for additional holes.

Can I use 5-gallon fabric pots indoors?

Yes, fabric pots work indoors with proper drainage trays underneath to protect floors from excess water runoff.
