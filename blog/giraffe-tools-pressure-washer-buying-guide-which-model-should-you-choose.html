Frequently Asked Questions

What PSI do I need for residential pressure washing?

For most residential tasks like cleaning patios and driveways, 1500-2000 PSI is sufficient. Giraffe Tools models typically range from 1200-2000+ PSI depending on the model, allowing you to select the right power for your specific needs.

Are Giraffe Tools pressure washers suitable for all surfaces?

Yes, with adjustable pressure settings and multiple nozzle tips, Giraffe Tools pressure washers can safely clean various surfaces including wood decks, vinyl siding, concrete, and painted surfaces when used correctly.

How do I maintain my Giraffe Tools pressure washer?

Electric Giraffe Tools models require minimal maintenance. Simply flush the system after use, store in a dry location, and inspect hoses and connections periodically. Unlike gas models, there's no engine oil to change or spark plugs to maintain.

Can I use a Giraffe Tools pressure washer for commercial cleaning?

While Giraffe Tools pressure washers excel at residential use, their lighter-duty models are best suited for home maintenance rather than heavy commercial applications requiring extended daily use.

What warranty do Giraffe Tools pressure washers come with?
