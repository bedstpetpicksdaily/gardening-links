Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best hand tools for beginners?

Beginners should start with essential hand tools including a trowel, hand fork, hand rake, and pruning shears. Garden Guru Tools offers beginner-friendly kits that include these basics with ergonomic handles designed to reduce learning curve and physical strain.

How often should I replace my garden tools?

With proper care and maintenance, quality garden tools from Garden Guru Tools can last 5-10 years or longer. Replace tools when blades become dull, handles crack, or rust develops that won't clean off. Regular sharpening and cleaning extend tool life significantly.

What's the difference between pruning shears and pruning saws?

Pruning shears handle smaller branches and stems up to ½ inch thick, while pruning saws are designed for thicker branches over 1 inch. Garden Guru Tools offers both options, and using the right tool for each job ensures clean cuts and plant health.

Are ergonomic handles worth the extra cost?

Yes, ergonomic handles significantly reduce hand fatigue and strain during extended gardening sessions. Garden Guru Tools prioritizes ergonomic design, making gardening more comfortable and reducing risk of repetitive strain injuries.

How do I maintain my garden tools for longevity?

Clean tools after each use, dry them thoroughly, sharpen blades regularly, and store in a dry location. Garden Guru Tools provides maintenance guides with their products, and these simple steps ensure your tools remain effective and rust-free for years.
