Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a typical seed starter tray kit?

Most seed starter tray kits include plastic seed trays, humidity domes, grow lights, light stands, and sometimes seed-starting soil or pellets.

How long should I leave grow lights on for seedlings?

Seedlings typically need 14-16 hours of light daily. Most kits come with timers to automate this process and ensure consistent growth.

Can I reuse seed starter trays?

Yes, seed trays are designed for repeated use. Simply clean them with a 10% bleach solution between seasons to prevent disease transmission.

When should I start seeds indoors?

Start seeds 6-8 weeks before your last spring frost date. Check your local frost date and count backward to determine the best starting time.

Do I need fertilizer for seeds in starter kits?

Seed-starting mix typically contains minimal nutrients. Begin light fertilizing once seedlings develop their true leaves using a diluted, balanced fertilizer.
